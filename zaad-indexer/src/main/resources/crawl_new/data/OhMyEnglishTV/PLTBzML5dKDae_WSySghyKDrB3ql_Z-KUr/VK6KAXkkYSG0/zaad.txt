Azlin announces that she has received an offer to act in a film alongside the popular actress, Lisa Surihani (as herself). Azlin brags non-stop about her new acting job, but refuses to allow anyone to come to the film set, except for Cikgu Malik (Aaron Aziz), whom she's appointed as her new manager. She gets upset when others from the school get invited to take part in the movie. Catch it this Sunday 9th June 2013 at 7:30pm on TVIQ Ch610 and MUSTIKA HD Ch134! Let's speak better English!
