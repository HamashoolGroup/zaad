Oh My English! Class of 2015 - "The Future" (Ep 19 Promo)
Published on Dec 21, 2015
The students are preparing for their exams. Some are busy studying whilst others are struggling with stress. Anusha, on the other hand, is moping around. Although she has finished her revision well in advance and is confident of good results, she doesn?t know what to do with her life after SPMs. While looking for answers, the students stumble upon the iconic Amber Chia, who gives them some priceless advice. Meanwhile, Cikgu Ayu is also contemplating her future in SMK Ayer Dalam and her on-off relationship with Henry. Cikgu Bedah urges her to make the first move since Henry thinks she is no longer interested. Para pelajar bersiap siaga menghadapi peperiksaan. Ada yang sibuk belajar dan ada yang mencari ketenangan. Bagaimanapun, Anusha kelihatan muram. Biarpun dia telah habis mengulang kaji dan yakin untuk mendapat keputusan cemerlang, Anusha tidak mempunyai cita-cita. Dalam kesibukan mencari jawapan, mereka terserempak dengan Amber Chia. Amber menasihati mereka cara menempatkan diri dalam kehidupan harian dan melakukannya secepat mungkin agar perjalanan hidup gemilang dan cemerlang.
