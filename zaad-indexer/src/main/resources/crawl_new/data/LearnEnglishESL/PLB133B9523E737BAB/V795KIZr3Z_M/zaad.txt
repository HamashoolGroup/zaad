Always enjoyable programs! - Teacher Frank ENGLISH LESSON 35 VIDEO TRANSCRIPT INTRODUCTION Pictures are of the "Middle Ages" (European history from 1000 - 1500). The story is about a knight and his lady. Lesson drawings from the Legend of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table. 1 Lesson Vocabulary Words And Phrases 2 What a dream I had pressed in organdy, "organdy" (n) 'a fine, translucent, stiff light cotton used chiefly for dresses' "The knight dreamt he was wrapped in organdy." 3 clothed in crinoline of smoky burgundy, softer than the rain. "crinoline" 'stiff horsehair thread' "burgundy" 'a deep red wine color; wine [Burgundy, Fr,] "In his dream, he had on a burgundy cloak." 4 I wandered empty streets down past the shop displays. "shop displays" (n) 'a collection of objects for sale shown in front of or inside the shops' "He walked straight past the shop displays." 5 I heard cathedral bells tripping down the alleyways as I walked on. "cathedral bells" (n) 'the bells of a major or principal church (i.e. cathedral)' "The large cathedral bells began ringing at first light." 6 And when you ran to me, your cheeks flushed with the night. "cheeks flushed" (v) 'face became light or red and hot, due to excitement or illness' "Her cheeks flushed when she saw her knight." 7 We walked on frosted fields of juniper and lamplight. I held your hand. "juniper" (n) 'an evergreen shrub or small tree bearing berry-like cones (a spice)' "The fields had juniper trees with bright blue berries." 8 "medieval" (adjective) 'relating to the Middle Ages; old-fashioned or outdated' "The musician is playing a lute, a medieval, stringed instrument." 9 "suit of armour" (n) 'metal coverings worn by soldiers to protect the body in battle' "Knights wore suits of armour (US armor) in medieval times." 10 "legend" (n) 'a traditional story accepted as history, but which is not proved' "English literature tells of the legend of King Arthur." 11 And when I awoke and felt you warm and near, I kissed your honey hair with my grateful tears. "grateful tears" (n) 'tears due to thankfulness; an appreciation of kindnesses' "He cried grateful tears that she had a love that was true." 12 Oh I love you, girl! Oh I love you!
