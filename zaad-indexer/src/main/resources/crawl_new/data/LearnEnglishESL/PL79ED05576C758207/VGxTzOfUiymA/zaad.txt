ENGLISH LISTENING PRACTICE AND EXERCISE 30 A. LISTENING CONTENT 1 A monologue is a long talk by one speaker. In this song the person speaks to the train - engine number 9. In the interactive classes, take notes about the meaning of the vocabulary items and phrases, and how they are used.
