Using Though At The End Of A Sentence | Like A Native Speaker
Published on Aug 14, 2014
Though is a useful contrastive conjunction! Most student learn to use it at the beginning or middle of a sentence, but never learn the most common way that Native Speakers use it: at the end of a sentence!
