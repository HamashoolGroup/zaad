General Extenders ...and Stuff | Like A Native Speaker
Published on Oct 16, 2014
General extenders are those little words you can put at the end of a sentence to sound much more natural and create a nicer flow to your words. 'And stuff', 'and things', 'or something' can all make you sound much more like a native speaker!
