Kalmyk topshur dueling (Viktor Batyrovich Okchayev and Dmitriy Sharayev)
Published on Jun 20, 2012
Dmitriy Sharayev and Viktor Batyrovich Okchayev (right) play the dombura (or topshur) together. The topshur is a Kalmyk two-stringed wooden instrument. Language: Kalmyk Location: Elista, Republic of Kalmykia, Russian Federation Date: 21 May 2012 Interviewer: K.
