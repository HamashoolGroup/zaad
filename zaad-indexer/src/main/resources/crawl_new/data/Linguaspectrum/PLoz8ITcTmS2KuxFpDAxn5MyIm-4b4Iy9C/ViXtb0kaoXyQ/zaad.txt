? The British English Expression - At the Drop of a Hat When you do something at the drop of a hat, you do it instantly and without hesitation. In a war, soldiers will kill other solders at the drop of a hat simply because they're the enemy. Most doctors prescribe drugs at the drop of a hat because their patients expect them to.
