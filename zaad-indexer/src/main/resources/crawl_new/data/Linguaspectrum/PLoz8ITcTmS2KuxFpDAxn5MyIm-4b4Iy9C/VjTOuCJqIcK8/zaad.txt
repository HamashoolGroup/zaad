? The British English Expression - The Lion's Share The lion's share is disproportionately the biggest part of something. She was beginning to regret marrying a man with such a hairy chest, the way he used the lion's share of the sun cream. His attempts to persuade his wife that it was only natural that she did the lion's share of the housework seemed to fall on deaf ears.
