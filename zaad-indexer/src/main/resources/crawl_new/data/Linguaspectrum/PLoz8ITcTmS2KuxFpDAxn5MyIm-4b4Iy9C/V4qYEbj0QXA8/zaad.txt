Fell Off the Back of a Lorry
Published on Jun 25, 2015
? The British English Expression - Fell off the back of a lorry. When you suspect that something was acquired dishonestly, you can say that it fell of the back of a lorry. I know they're not cheap, but at this price, well, you can be sure they didn't fall off the back of a lorry. Designer handbags at what price? Well, they must have fallen off the back of a lorry.
