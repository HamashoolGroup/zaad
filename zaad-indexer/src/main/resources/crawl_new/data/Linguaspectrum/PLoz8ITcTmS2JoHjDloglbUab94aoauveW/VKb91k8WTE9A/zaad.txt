What's Going On? Maps - Learn English
Published on Aug 7, 2015
To get the most from this video English lesson, watch the interactive video at http://wp.me/p6tmHQ-6ZW This video English lesson asks you to complete 4 statements about maps choosing the correct answer from those given. The video can be watched on its own and will provide full answers at the end. However, at Linguaspectrum you get several more questions to complete to make the interactive video more interesting and useful. The lesson looks at the following vocabulary: reading, looking, seeing, the way, instructions, directions, get lost, lose yourself, end point, destination, place, and provides practice for using each of these vocabulary items.
