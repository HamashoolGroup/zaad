? Phrasal Verb Library: http://linguaspectrum.com/phrasal-ver... ? The English Phrasal Verb - Take Up She took up kick-boxing after some perishing fellow tried to steal her handbag. She took kick-boxing up after some perishing fellow tried to mug her in the street. At first, I didn't think she was serious about taking up kick-boxing. To take up means to begin doing some kind of activity or sport. This phrasal verb is transitive and separable.
