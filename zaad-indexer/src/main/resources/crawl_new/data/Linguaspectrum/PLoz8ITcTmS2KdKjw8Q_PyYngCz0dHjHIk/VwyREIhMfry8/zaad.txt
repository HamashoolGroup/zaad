? The English Phrasal Verb - Stick With It's hard to learn to ride a bike, but if you stick with it, you'll eventually get it. She found riding a bike challenging at first, but she stuck with it and mastered it. By sticking with it, she eventually learnt to ride a bicycle. If you stick with something, you keep doing it however difficult it may be. This phrasal verb is transitive and inseparable.
