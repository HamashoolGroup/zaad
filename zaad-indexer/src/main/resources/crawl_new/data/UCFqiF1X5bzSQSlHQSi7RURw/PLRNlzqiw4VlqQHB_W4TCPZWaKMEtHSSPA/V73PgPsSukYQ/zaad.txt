In today's video, I broke down for you the SAT Vocabulary word, BENEVOLENT, to help you memorize it. Here is the definition for the word: BENEVOLENT (adj): Well meaning and kindly Synonyms: Kind, good, kindhearted, compassionate, caring Sentence: The benevolent elderly lady gave all her money away to The Alzheimer's Foundation. The key idea with being able to quickly memorize english vocabulary words is the create pictures for those words. The Pictures for this SAT Vocabulary Word is: Benevolent: Bone Envelope with Lint Definition: Kind Dog Then just take both the picture for the word and the picture for the definition and visualize an interaction between the two. Story: Dog got an Envelope in the mail with a Bone for being very Kind to its owner That's It! Just review a little bit and you will have these words fully memorized come test day.
