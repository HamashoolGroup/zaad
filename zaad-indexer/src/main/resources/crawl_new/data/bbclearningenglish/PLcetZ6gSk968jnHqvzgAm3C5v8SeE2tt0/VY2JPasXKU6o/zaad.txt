Stop Saying: Answering negative questions
Published on Jan 22, 2016
When a yes/no question is asked with a negative, it can be difficult to know what the correct way to respond is. Anita from Italy wants to know that if someone asks "Aren't you going out tonight?" what the right answer is. Does the speaker think you are or are not going out? If you say yes, does this mean that you are not going out? If you say no, does it mean you are going out? It seems very confusing! Thankfully Tim has the answer and it's very simple.
