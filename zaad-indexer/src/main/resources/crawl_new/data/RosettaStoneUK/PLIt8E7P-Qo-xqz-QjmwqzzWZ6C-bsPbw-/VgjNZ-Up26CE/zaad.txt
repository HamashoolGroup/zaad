Published on Nov 14, 2012
Rosetta Stone Public Sector Seminar 2012 Rosetta Stone provided a platform for leaders in public sector and higher education space to debate the current linguistic skills gap and its impact on organisations and the economy. Examples from some leading public sector organisations showed how employers can set up a language training programme to their benefit and that of their staff.
