Quitting smoking before 44-years-old erases risk of early death (Slow Easy English News)
Published on Jan 25, 2013
A new study shows that lifelong smokers live about 10 years less than non-smokers, but those who quite before age 44 can get back almost all of that time - 9 years. The risk of lung cancer, however, remains.
