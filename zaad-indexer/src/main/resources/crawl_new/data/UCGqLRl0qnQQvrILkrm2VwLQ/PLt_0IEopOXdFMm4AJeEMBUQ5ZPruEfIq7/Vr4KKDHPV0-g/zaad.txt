BBC Learning English - 6 Minute English '14: Showrooming and shopping
Published on Jan 20, 2016
Source: http://www.bbc.co.uk/worldservice/lea... Have you ever visited a shop to see and test a product but then gone and bought it cheaper online? If so, you have been 'showrooming' - a new word to describe this activity. In 6 Minute English, Rob and Finn talk about why people do this and how it is affecting the shops in our town centres. They'll also be explaining some of the vocabulary related to shopping.
