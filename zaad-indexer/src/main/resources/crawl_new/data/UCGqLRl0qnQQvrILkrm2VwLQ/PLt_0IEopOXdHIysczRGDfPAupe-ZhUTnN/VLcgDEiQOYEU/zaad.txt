English at Work 10: The brainstorm - BBC Learning English
Published on Feb 9, 2016
The team at Tip Top trading are hard at work preparing for the launch and presentation of their latest product - the Imperial Lemon. Tom, Anna and Denise get together to share their ideas - something we call brainstorming. Anna's ideas are met with some negative comments and she has to learn how to disagree with the other's viewpoint in a confident but polite manner. Language for disagreeing This week's programme focuses on language to use when you want to disagree with somebody else's ideas and put forward your viewpoint.
