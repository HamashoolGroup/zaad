Decisions Change Your Life
Published on Apr 13, 2015
Decisions are power. Decisions change your life. What kind of decisions must you make to become an excellent English speaker? How do you make these decisions and how do you achieve the results you want? AJ tells you how in this Effortless English Show. In the Show, he discusses his upcoming trip to Spain to walk the Camino de Santiago. He talks about the power of one decision. One decision can have a "ripple effect" through your entire life. When you decide to change the way you learn English, you are making a decision that will change your life. This decision may influence your future jobs, income, travel, friendships, and career.
