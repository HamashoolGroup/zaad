Great Teachers and Tutors
Published on Mar 18, 2015
How Do You Find A Great English Teacher, Tutor, or Conversation Partner? What kind of person will help you succeed at the highest possible level? In this Show, AJ talks about the qualities of a good teacher. A good teacher can change your English and change your life. They inspire you to improve, to achieve your goals, and to succeed. A bad teacher can destroy your confidence and ruin your success. You must choose a teacher or a class very carefully. Sadly, most schools and most teachers are mediocre at best. Do your research and find the very best teacher for you.
