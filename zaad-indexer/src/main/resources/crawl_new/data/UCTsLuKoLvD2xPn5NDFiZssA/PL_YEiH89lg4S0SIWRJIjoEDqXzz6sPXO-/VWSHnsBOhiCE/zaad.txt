Business English Power Verbs - an app for improving your business English
Published on Jan 16, 2014
The Business English Power Verbs app will help you improve your Business English, in a fun and interactive way! It's available now on iTunes. Sound better when you speak English by mastering verbs like leverage, tout, exhort, and skyrocket. Listen to each verb and repeat after speaker, a native of the USA. Two sentence examples are given for each verb -- complete with audio so you can listen and repeat. Two sets of exercises let you test your new knowledge and get immediate feedback. - Master 101 key business English verbs - Listen to proper pronunciation - Read and hear realistic usage examples - Take quizzes and get immediate feedback - Have fun while learning!
