Wie sagt man auf Englisch FITNESS STUDIO?
Published on Mar 2, 2015
If you want to impress an English speaking woman or man, you could tell them about how sporty you are. Just don't say to them: "I go to the fitness studio!". You might end up not impressing them that much, because FITNESS STUDIO doesn't exist in English. Watch the video and I'll explain how you can say this and more.
