Wie sagt man die Zahl "Null" auf Englisch? Business English lesson
Published on Oct 20, 2014
This business English video tip explains how you can say "Null" in English. There are different ways to say it and this can be confusing. You can say oh, zero, nought, love or nil. It depends on if the "O" is part of a telephone number, the time, a year, a decimal, a number or a score in football or tennis.
