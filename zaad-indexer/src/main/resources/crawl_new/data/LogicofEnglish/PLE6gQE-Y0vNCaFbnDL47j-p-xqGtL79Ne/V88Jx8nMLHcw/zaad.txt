Reading & Spelling Teacher Training Section 2
Published on Sep 22, 2012
Laying the Foundations for Reading: Phonemic Awareness and Phonograms. In Section 2 Denise Eide provides teachers with the tools to lay a strong foundation for reading. By drawing on early childhood development she demonstrates the importance of explicit teaching in phonemic awareness and phonograms for preparing students to read. Denise provides educators with clear steps and fun games for developing phonemic awareness, learning to blend and segment words by ear, and introducing phonograms.
