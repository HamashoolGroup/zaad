Basic English Lesson : How to talk about past actions? | Grammar Lessons - Simple past tense
Published on Jan 22, 2013
How to talk about actions? Simple Past tense : We use this tense in English to talk about events or situations that are finished. Normally we use a time reference. For example: "Last year I went to Spain." "In 1997, he changed his job." "She called an hour ago." In this lesson Ceema explains how to talk about your daily activities in simple past tense.
