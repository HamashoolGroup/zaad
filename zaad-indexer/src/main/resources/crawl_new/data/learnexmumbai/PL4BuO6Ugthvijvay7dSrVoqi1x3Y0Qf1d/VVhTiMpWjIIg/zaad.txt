The conjunctions and and but can be used to join two statements.In this lessons you will learn how to use the conjunctions 'and & 'but' correctly. And: shows addition and is used when the statements are similar. I called Jan and we talked for an hour. But: is used to connect statements that express opposite ideas. I also called Steve, but he wasn't home.
