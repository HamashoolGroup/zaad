Listen and Read News ? UN deal to track airliners hopes to avoid another missing jet like MH370
Published on Nov 12, 2015
The disappearance of Malaysia Airlines flight MH370 left one major question on everyone's lips: with all of today's technologies, how could a jetliner just disappear? A UN deal struck today in Geneva between nations promises that that shouldn't happen again. The agreement opens the way for real-time satellite tracking of airliners.
