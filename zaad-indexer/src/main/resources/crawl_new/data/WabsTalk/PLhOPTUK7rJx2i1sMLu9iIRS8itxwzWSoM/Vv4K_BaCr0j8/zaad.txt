Introduction to IELTS
Published on Jun 17, 2013
http://wabstalk.com IELTS helps you to improve your communication skills in English language. Many countries have made IELTS exam clearance compulsory if you want to work or study there. Full form of IELTS is: International English Language Testing System. This test is formulated to assess the language ability of candidates. IELTS is recognised by universities, government agencies, employers in countries like UK, Canada, Ireland, New Zealand, the UK, the USA and Australia.
