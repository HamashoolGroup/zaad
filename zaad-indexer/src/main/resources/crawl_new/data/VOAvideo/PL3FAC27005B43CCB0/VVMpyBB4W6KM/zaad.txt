Texas-Based Nigerian Filmmaker Explores Tardiness as Cultural Rift
Published on Aug 27, 2014
Many Africans have left their homelands to find opportunities abroad, but they carry with them cultural traditions that can create problems. One example is tardiness, an issue tackled by Texas-based Nigerian filmmaker Kelechi Eke, who makes ?Nollywood? style films that focus on the lives of African immigrants.
