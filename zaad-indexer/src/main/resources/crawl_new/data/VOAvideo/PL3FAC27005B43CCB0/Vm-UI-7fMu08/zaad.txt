Worldwide Photo Workshops Empower Youth
Published on Jan 23, 2015
Last September, 20 young adults from South Sudan took part in a National Geographic Photo Camp. They are among hundreds of students from around the world who have learned how to use a camera to tell the stories of the people in their communities through the powerful medium of photography.
