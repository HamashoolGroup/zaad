As Obama Greets Pope, Poor Workers Hope for Help
Published on Sep 22, 2015
Ahead of Pope Francis' meeting with President Barack Obama at the White House and his speech to Congress, low-wage contract workers who cook and clean for U.S. lawmakers walked off their jobs at the Capitol, asking for a $15 minimum wage and a union.
