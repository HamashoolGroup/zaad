Special Olympics Prompts Call for More Inclusive Treatment of Disabled
Published on Jul 28, 2015
Athletes from around the world are competing this week in Los Angeles at the Special Olympics World Games. Heads of global organizations attending the event are calling for changes to ensure those who are intellectually disabled are not excluded by society. VOA's Elizabeth Lee reports.
