Syrian Refugees Given New Hope for Education in Lebanon
Published on Sep 23, 2015
With a half-million school age Syrian refugees in Lebanon, many are deprived of an education. But as school gets set to begin, the Lebanese government is embarking on a $94 million educational drive to give thousands of youngsters the chance of a better future.
