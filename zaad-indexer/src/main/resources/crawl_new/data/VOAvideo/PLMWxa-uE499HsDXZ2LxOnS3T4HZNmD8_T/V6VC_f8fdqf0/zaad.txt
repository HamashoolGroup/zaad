Croatia Begins Busing Migrants Direct from Serbia Border to Hungary
Published on Sep 24, 2015
As thousands more refugees and migrants pour into Croatia, authorities have begun busing them directly to its border with Hungary. The fast-track move may ease pressure in Croatia but could also encourage other nations on the migration route to do the same.
