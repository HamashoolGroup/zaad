Intense Migrant Flow Puts Pressure on Germany
Published on Sep 17, 2015
Germany, the destination of choice for many of the migrants entering Europe, is facing questions on how it will deal with the influx. Refugees have been entering at a rate of 1,800 per day from the Austrian border, despite border restrictions enforced since Sunday. VOA Europe correspondent Luis Ramirez reports from Munich.
