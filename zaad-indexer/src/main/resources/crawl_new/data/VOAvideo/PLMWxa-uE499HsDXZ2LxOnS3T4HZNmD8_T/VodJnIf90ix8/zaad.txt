Volunteers Working to Bring Relief to Desperate Migrants
Published on Sep 14, 2015
Even as Hungary clamps down on the flow of refugees and migrants trying to enter the country, volunteers have gathered in Hungarian cities to help those who have already arrived. VOA Afghan service reporters Hoshang Fahim and Fawad Lami have more from one of the holding points at Budapest's main railway station, in this report narrated by Robert Raffaele.
