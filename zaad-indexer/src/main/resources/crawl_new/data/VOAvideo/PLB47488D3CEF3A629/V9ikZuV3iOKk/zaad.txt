Rep. Howard "Buck" McKeon on Obama's Foreign Policy
Published on Aug 4, 2014
On this edition of the program, host Carol Castiel and VOA congressional correspondent Cindy Saine talk with Congressman Howard ?Buck? McKeon, a Republican from the state of California and chairman of the influential House Armed Services Committee about a series of international and domestic issues including the civil conflicts raging in Iraq and Syria, the Gaza-Israel war, the downing of Malaysian flight 17 over eastern Ukraine, and the influx of migrant minors from Central America into the United States.
