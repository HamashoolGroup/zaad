Karen Donfried on EU Sanctions & Anti Putin Dissent Inside Russia
Published on Oct 3, 2014
Carol Castiel, host of Press Conference USA, speaks with Karen Donfried, President of the German Marshall Fund in Washington, formally director of European affairs at the National Security Council in the Obama administration, about the crisis in Ukraine and the prospects of the current truce holding between Kiev and Moscow backed separatist rebels in eastern Ukraine, as well as about the challenges ahead for Ukraine, including parliamentary elections, combating corruption and implementation of other reforms.
