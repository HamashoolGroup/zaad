Laurie Garrett on the Biggest Challenges Right Now
Published on Oct 10, 2014
On this edition of the program, host Carol Castiel speaks with Laurie Garrett, Ebola expert and Senior Fellow for Global Health at the Council on Foreign Relations, about the initial failures of the international community in detecting and stopping the spread of the deadly Ebola virus. She not only discusses the causes of the slow response, but also the various political, economic, cultural, and social factors that may hinder efforts by the United States, the UN and others to contain the epidemic.
