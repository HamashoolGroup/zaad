Obama Addresses Security, Gay Rights in Kenya
Published on Jul 26, 2015
Kenya gave U.S. President Barack Obama a warm welcome on his first full day in the country on a two-day visit. Mr. Obama met with Kenyan President Uhuru Kenyatta, and the two leaders said they were united on security issues and the need to improve cooperation. However, some issues still divided them.
