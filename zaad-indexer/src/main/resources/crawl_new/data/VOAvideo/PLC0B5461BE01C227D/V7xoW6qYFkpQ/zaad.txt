Asia Seeks to Improve Vital Birth Registrations
Published on Nov 25, 2014
This week government ministers from Southeast Asia are meeting in Bangkok to discuss how to improve official registrations of births, deaths and marriages - critical measures for providing government services. Thailand has had notable success but issues remain, especially with birth registrations for children of migrant workers. VOA Correspondent Steve Herman reports from Samut Prakan.
