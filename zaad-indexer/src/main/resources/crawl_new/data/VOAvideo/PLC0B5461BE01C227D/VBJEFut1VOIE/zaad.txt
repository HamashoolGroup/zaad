At Least 2 Dead as Typhoon Batters Philippines
Published on Dec 7, 2014
Typhoon Hagupit centered over the eastern Philippines Sunday bringing heavy rain and wind gusts at times as high 210 kilometers per hour. VOA's Brian Padden was on the centrally located island of Cebu, which, as he reports, has escaped the brunt of the storm's destructive force.
