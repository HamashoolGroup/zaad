US, China Highlight Cooperation as Differences Loom Large
Published on Nov 12, 2014
China and the United States have jointly announced what they are calling a major agreement on climate change. The agreement was reached following talks in Beijing between U.S. President Barack Obama and his Chinese counterpart Xi Jinping. The two also pledged to work together on a wide range of issues, but differences between the two countries still loom large. VOA?s Bill Ide has more from Beijing.
