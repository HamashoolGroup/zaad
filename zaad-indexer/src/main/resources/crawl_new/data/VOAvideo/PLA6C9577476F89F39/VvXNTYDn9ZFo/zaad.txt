Snakehead Fish Fair Game for Washington Anglers
Published on Jun 7, 2012
An Asian invader is threatening to take over the waters outside Washington. An invasive species of fish called the snakehead arrived here about a decade ago, and its numbers have been growing ever since. Now, wildlife officials are taking an unusual step to blunt the invasion and protect native species. They are encouraging fishermen to fish the snakeheads -- to extinction. VOA's Steve Baragona has more.
