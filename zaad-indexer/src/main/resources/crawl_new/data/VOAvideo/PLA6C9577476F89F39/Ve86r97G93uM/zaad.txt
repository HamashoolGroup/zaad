Goldman Environmental Prizes Honor Diverse Group of Activists
Published on Apr 26, 2012
A Catholic priest, a tribal leader and a mother were among the winners of this year's prestigious Goldman Environmental Prize. The privately-sponsored award honors outstanding grassroots environmental activists from six different regions of the world. This year, the laureates are from the Philippines, Kenya, China, Russia, Argentina and the United States. VOA's Deborah Block has their stories.
