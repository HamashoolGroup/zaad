UN Warns of Irreversible Damage to Planet
Published on Jun 7, 2012
A United Nations report says the world faces irreversible change to life support functions if humanity does not change its ways. The United Nations Environment Program (UNEP) issued the report ahead the U.N. Conference on Sustainable Development, set for Brazil at the end of June. VOA Zulima Palacio has the story.
