Baltimore Residents Demand Answers after Detainee's Death
Published on Apr 22, 2015
Six police officers in the eastern U.S. state of Maryland are on paid leave as investigators look into what killed a 25-year-old man in police custody. Residents in the Baltimore neighborhood where Freddie Gray was arrested tell VOA's Victoria Macchi they want answers for what they say is a longstanding face-off between police and the black community.
