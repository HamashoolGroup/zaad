LA Rescue Crew Heads to Nepal
Published on Apr 27, 2015
In the wake of the massive earthquake in Nepal, search and rescue teams from around the world are converging on the capital, Kathmandu, to help the survivors. One of the teams is a unit of the Los Angeles Fire Department in the western U.S. state of California. Jeff Shu has more in this report narrated by Elizabeth Arrott.
