Obama: Strong US-Japan Alliance Not Provocation to China
Published on Apr 28, 2015
President Barack Obama says a strong alliance between the United States and Japan should not be seen by China as a provocation. Obama made the comment as he welcomed Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe for a state visit during which the two leaders discussed bolstering security and economic ties.
