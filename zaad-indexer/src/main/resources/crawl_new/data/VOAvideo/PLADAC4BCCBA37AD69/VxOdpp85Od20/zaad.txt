Gadhafi Has Mixed Legacy in Sub-Saharan Africa
Uploaded on Aug 29, 2011
While embattled Libyan leader Moammar Gadhafi has poured a great deal of investment and development money into sub-Saharan Africa over the years, his legacy in the region is mixed. Some say he will also be remembered for spending a lot of money to foment revolutions on the continent.
