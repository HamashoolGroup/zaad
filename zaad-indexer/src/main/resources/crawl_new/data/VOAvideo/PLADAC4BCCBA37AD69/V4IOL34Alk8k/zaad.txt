Niger Food Shortages Continue
Uploaded on Oct 18, 2010
Strong rains are boosting hopes for a good harvest in Niger, where nearly half the people do not have enough to eat. Additionally, the strength of the international community's response to this year's food crisis in Niger has helped feed more than 220,000 children under the age of five. Yet relief officials already are predicting that the food crisis will continue into next year because the need remains so fierce.
