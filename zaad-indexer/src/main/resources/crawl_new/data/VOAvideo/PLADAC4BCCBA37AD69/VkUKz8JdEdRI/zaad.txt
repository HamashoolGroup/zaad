US Deficit Reduction Committee Fails
Uploaded on Nov 22, 2011
A special U.S. congressional committee has failed in its mission to craft a bipartisan plan to reduce the federal deficit by at least $1.2 trillion over the next 10 years. VOA Senate correspondent Michael Bowman reports, months of negotiations could not bridge partisan ideological differences on how to improve America's fiscal health.
