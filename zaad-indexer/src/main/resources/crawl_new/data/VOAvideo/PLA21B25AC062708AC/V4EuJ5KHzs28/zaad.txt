Women Roman Catholic Priests
Uploaded on Apr 11, 2008
As Pope Benedict prepares to travel to the United States, he continues to uphold the church's long-standing tradition of not ordaining women as priests. Since July 2006, more than 20 women in the U.S. with the organization Roman Catholic Womenpriests have considered themselves ordained without church approval. VOA's Jeff Swicord reports on one woman in Sarasota, Florida who says it is time for the church to openly accept women into the priesthood.
