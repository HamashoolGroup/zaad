U.S Cites OIC as Weakening Religious Protections
Uploaded on Sep 22, 2008
A newly issued report from the U.S. State Department says an intergovernmental organization of Islamic countries has worked to weaken protections of religious freedom. The annual report also lists countries whose repression of religion concerns the U.S. -- particularly North Korea and Burma. VOA's Robert Raffaele has more.
