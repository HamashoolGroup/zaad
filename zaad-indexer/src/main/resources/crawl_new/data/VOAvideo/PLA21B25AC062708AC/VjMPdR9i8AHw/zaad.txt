Jerusalem Pilgrimage is a Moment of Sacrifice
Uploaded on Apr 13, 2009
Thousands of Christian Pilgrims have filled the streets of Jerusalem's Old City for Holy Week celebrations. For many, the trip is a sacrifice at a time of worldwide recession. Christian celebrations coincide this year with the Jewish festival of Passover, which has also drawn Jews to the Holy City. As VOA's Jerusalem correspondent Luis Ramirez reports, pilgrims of various faiths use the occasion to pray for peace in the Middle East.
