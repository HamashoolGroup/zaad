Farrow's War Crimes Testimony Contradicts Naomi Campbell
Uploaded on Aug 9, 2010
American Actress Mia Farrow testified Monday at the war crimes trial of former Liberian President Charles Taylor. She told the court at The Hague that supermodel Naomi Campbell claimed in1997 to have been given a large diamond by Mr. Taylor after a dinner party hosted by Nelson Mandela. As Selah Hennessy reports from London, Farrow's testimony contradicts Campbell's own evidence given last week.
