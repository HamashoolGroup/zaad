NY Islamic Center Protests Continue with Conflicting Views of Freedom
Uploaded on Aug 23, 2010
Opponents of the proposed Islamic center near the site of the 2001 terrorist attacks on New York City gathered near the site on Sunday to rally against the project. Supporters gathered two blocks away, while a national television audience listened to an organizer explain the purpose of the proposed center. VOA New York Correspondent Peter Fedynsky reports that the differing positions are based on each side's view of American liberty.
