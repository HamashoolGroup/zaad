Haitian Faithful Attend Mass at Earthquake-Shattered Cathedral
Uploaded on Jan 18, 2010
Drumbeats called the faithful to what is left of Port-au-Prince's main cathedral for a somber Mass Sunday, the first since the devastating earthquake. The Cathedral, once a symbol of hope for Haiti now lies in ruins and parishioners are vowing to rebuild it again. VOA's Jeff Swicord reports from Port-au-Prince.
