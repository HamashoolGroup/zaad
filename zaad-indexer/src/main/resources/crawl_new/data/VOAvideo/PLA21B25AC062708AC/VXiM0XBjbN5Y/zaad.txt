Obama Seeks to Bridge Differences with Islam
Uploaded on Mar 19, 2010
US President Barack Obama has again postponed his trip to Indonesia because of pressing issues in the U.S. The trip is part of his ongoing effort to reach out to Muslims worldwide. Last year, he gave a speech in Cairo that was considered a landmark in the effort to bridge differences between Islam and the West. VOA's Jerome Socolovsky reports that many Muslims believe he should do more.
