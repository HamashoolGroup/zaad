Same Sex Marriage on Encounter
Published on May 23, 2012
What are the political, social and legal ramifications of same sex marriage in the United States? Host Carol Castiel explores the controversial question with Jo Deutsch of the Freedom to Marry, an advocate of same sex marriage, and Janice Crouse of the Concerned Women for America, an opponent of same sex marriage, in the wake of President Barack Obama's watershed statement endorsing the right of gay and lesbian couples to marry.
