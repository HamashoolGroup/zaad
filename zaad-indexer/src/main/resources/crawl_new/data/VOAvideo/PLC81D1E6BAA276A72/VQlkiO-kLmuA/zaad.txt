An Hour Workout A Day Helps Keep Extra Weight Away for Older Women
Uploaded on Mar 30, 2010
Here is sobering news. The World Health Organization says more than one billion adults are fat, and at least 400 million people are obese. As old fashioned as it sounds, doctors say the best way to lose weight is to eat less and exercise more. But for women who are middle aged or older, experts say one hour of daily exercise per day is necessary just to maintain weight. As VOA's Melinda Smith reports, the heavier a woman is, the harder she will have to work -- at cutting calories.
