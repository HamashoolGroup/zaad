US Court Finds No Link Between Vaccines, Autism
Uploaded on Mar 23, 2010
For decades some parents, researchers and doctors have debated a possible link between autism and vaccines that contain the preservative thimerosal. Thousands of parents with autistic children took their case to court when their children developed autism after being vaccinated. But a U.S. federal court says it sees no evidence of a connection. VOA's Vidushi Sinha has a report, narrated by Carol Pearson.
