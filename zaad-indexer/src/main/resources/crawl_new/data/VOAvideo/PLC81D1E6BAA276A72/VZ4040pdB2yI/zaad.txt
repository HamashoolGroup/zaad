Health Officials Learn From H1N1 Pandemic
Uploaded on Apr 29, 2010
A full year has passed since epidemic experts began tracking the H1N1 virus that was also known as swine flu. The pandemic is over for now, but the virus is still active. Both the World Health Organization and the U.S. Centers for Disease Control are reviewing their responses. VOA's Carol Pearson has more.
