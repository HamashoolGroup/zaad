US Hospital Ship Mercy Sets Off on Pacific Mission
Uploaded on May 6, 2010
The US Navy hospital ship Mercy set off recently (Saturday 5/1) on a humanitarian mission to the Western Pacific and Southeast Asia. It carries military and civilian medical workers who will offer treatment to patients in Vietnam, Cambodia, Indonesia and East Timor. Mike O'Sullivan reports from San Diego, California.
