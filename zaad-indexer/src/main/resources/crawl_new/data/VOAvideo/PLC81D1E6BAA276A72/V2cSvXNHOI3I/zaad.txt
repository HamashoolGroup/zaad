Health Study Warns Against Canned Food Coated with BPA
Uploaded on May 20, 2010
A new study by a coalition of environmental health advocates says common canned foods can have alarming levels of BPA, a chemical widely linked to numerous health problems. The National Workgroup for Safe Markets, and U.S. Senator Dianne Feinstein, released the study earlier this week (Monday, May 17) at a news conference in Washington. Producer Zulima Palacio covered the event. Elizabeth Lee narrates.
