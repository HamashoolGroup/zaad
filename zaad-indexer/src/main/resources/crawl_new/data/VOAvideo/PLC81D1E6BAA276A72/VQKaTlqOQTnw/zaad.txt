Antibiotic Resistance Is A Growing Threat
Uploaded on May 18, 2010
Resistance to antibiotics has become a growing concern among health experts worldwide. Experts fear that in a few years we might become nearly defenseless against some bacterial infections. VOA's Vidushi Sinha has a report on the emerging public health dilemma on the use of high powered drugs that seem to trigger greater resistance among bacteria.
