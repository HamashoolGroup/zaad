California Center Offers Support, Permanent Housing for Mentally-Ill Homeless
Uploaded on Dec 27, 2010
Many of the chronically homeless suffer from mental illness, and experts say that psychiatric treatment is an important step in resolving their homelessness. The challenge is -- finding a place for them to live and getting them into treatment. Mike O'Sullivan reports on an innovative program that is placing the homeless in permanent housing in a supportive community.
