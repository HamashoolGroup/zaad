Back On My Feet
Uploaded on Jun 13, 2011
Back on my Feet is a non-profit organization that works with people living in homeless shelters -- many addicted to drugs and alcohol. The group sponsors run near the shelters to help those in need improve their health and self esteem. Back on my Feet has branches in several cities in the United States. Deborah Block reports.
