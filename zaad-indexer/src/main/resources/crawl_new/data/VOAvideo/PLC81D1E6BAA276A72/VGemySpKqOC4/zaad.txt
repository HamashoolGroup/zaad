Haitian Soccer Amputees Assist Wounded US Soldiers
Uploaded on Oct 21, 2011
Members of a Haitian amputee soccer team were in Washington this week to conduct clinics for wounded American troops from Walter Reed National Military Medical Center. VOA's Parke Brewer reports it was the Haitians' way of saying thank you to the U.S. military for providing assistance to Haiti after the devastating earthquake last year.
