Breast, Cervical Cancer on the Rise
Uploaded on Sep 29, 2011
Women all over the world are at increased risk of developing breast and cervical cancers. A new study finds that, for reasons not yet understood, the incidence of these cancers is up in most countries, with the rise especially sharp in the developing world. And as Carol Pearson reports, young women are not immune from the heightened cancer risk.
