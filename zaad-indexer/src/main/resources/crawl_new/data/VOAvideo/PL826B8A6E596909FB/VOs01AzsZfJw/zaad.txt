Syrian Refugees Brace for Harsh Lebanon Winter
Published on Oct 28, 2014
As Syrian refugees in Lebanon brace for what is expected to be a harsh winter, analysts say the country's borders are at risk from Islamic State and al-Nusra fighters camped in the mountains. But refugees in nearby valleys say the coming cold ? not a militant incursion ? is the real enemy. Heather Murdock has more for VOA from Zahle, Lebanon.
