Israeli-Palestinian Tensions High Following Attacks
Published on Nov 11, 2014
Tensions are running high in Israel and the Palestinian territories following a series of fatal attacks. The violence was sparked by an escalating dispute over holy sites in Jerusalem but is also being blamed on new Israeli construction in the West Bank and failed attempts to revive the Israeli-Palestinian peace process.
