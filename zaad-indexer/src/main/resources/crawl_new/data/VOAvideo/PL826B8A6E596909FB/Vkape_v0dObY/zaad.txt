Kobani Residents Return to Widespread Devastation
Published on Feb 2, 2015
Residents of the Syrian town of Kobani are returning to their devastated homes, after months of fighting between the Islamic State and Kurdish fighters supported by U.S.-led airstrikes. Khadijan Farquin, a reporter for VOA's Kurdish Service, toured the battle-scarred town and filed this report, which is narrated by Elizabeth Arrott.
