Victorious Secularists Face Challenge to Form Government in Tunisia
Published on Oct 30, 2014
Official results from Tunisia show the Islamist Ennahda party has failed to win the second free election since the so-called "Arab Spring" uprising in 2011. Ennahda, which handed power to a government of technocrats pending the elections, lost out to the secular party Nidaa Tounes.
