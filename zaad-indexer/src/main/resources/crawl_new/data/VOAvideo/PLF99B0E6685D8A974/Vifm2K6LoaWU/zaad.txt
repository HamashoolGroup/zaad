Women's S. Africa Cross-Border Trade Fair Is Successful
Published on Sep 8, 2015
In hopes of empowering women, South Africa and Zimbabwe organized a cross-border trade fair last week for women operating small and medium-sized businesses. The event in Musina, South Africa, attracted more than 600 women who exhibited a wide range of products. Thuso Khumalo reports for VOA from Musina.
