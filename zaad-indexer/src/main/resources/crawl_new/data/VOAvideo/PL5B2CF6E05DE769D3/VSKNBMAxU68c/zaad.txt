Islamophobia Persists in Some Sectors of American Society
Uploaded on Sep 9, 2010
Reverend Terry Jones' now cancelled plan to burn copies of the Quran at his small church in Flordia, and the recent public outcry against plans to build an Islamic center in New York City, underscores what some see as heightened fear and animosity toward Muslims and Islam in the United States. As VOA's Michael Bowman reports from Washington, so-called "Islamophobia" clearly does exist, but more on the fringes of U.S. society than in the mainstream.
