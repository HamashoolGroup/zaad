Lawmakers Blast Failure of Planned "Virtual Fence" along US-Mexico border
Uploaded on Apr 22, 2010
Lawmakers in Washington say U.S. and Mexican authorities need much more help to battle violent drug traffickers. At a hearing this week, several members of a Senate committee blasted plans for a so-called "virtual fence" as a waste of taxpayer dollars. And two lawmakers are calling for the deployment of National Guard troops along one of the most troubled sections of the U.S. southern border. VOA's Robert Raffaele has more.
