Women, Girls in Nairobi Learn How to Prevent Rape
Uploaded on Apr 30, 2010
Rape is a constant threat for girls and women living in the crime-ridden slums of Kenya's capital, Nairobi. It is estimated that one out of five women are raped in the slums, and there are few arrests of rapists. A U.S.-initiated and funded program is working hard to change all that. Cathy Majtenyi reports for VOA from Nairobi.
