Thai Army Storms Protester Camp
Uploaded on May 20, 2010
The Thai army Wednesday stormed a barricaded protest camp in Bangkok. The Red Shirt protesters engaged the military, firing grenades and setting fires throughout the city. At least two protesters and an Italian news photographer were killed. The Red Shirt leadership surrendered in the afternoon but violence has not ended. VOA's Brian Padden was with the Thai military and files this report.
