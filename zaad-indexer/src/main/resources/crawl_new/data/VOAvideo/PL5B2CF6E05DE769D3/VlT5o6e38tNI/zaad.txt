US Lawmakers to Grill Goldman Sachs Executives
Uploaded on Apr 28, 2010
Top executives at Goldman Sachs will face tough questions on Capitol Hill Tuesday. A Senate subcommittee is looking into the actions of the investment firm following allegations by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (S.E.C.) that company executives made huge profits by betting against their own investments. Company executives say they did nothing wrong, but lawmakers say internal company email prove the company misled investors. Mil Arcega has more.
