Americans Hoping to Bounce Back After Loss to Italy
Uploaded on Jun 18, 2009
The U.S. men's soccer team is set to play its second match in FIFA's eight-nation Confederations Cup tournament in South Africa Thursday. After losing 3-1 to reigning World Cup champion Italy, it does not get any easier for the Americans. They face five-time World Cup champion Brazil Thursday in Pretoria. VOA Sports Editor Parke Brewer is there.
