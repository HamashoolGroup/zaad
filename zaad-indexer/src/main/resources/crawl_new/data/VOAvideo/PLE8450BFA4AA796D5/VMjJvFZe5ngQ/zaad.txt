US Olympians Reflect on Journey to Beijing Games
Uploaded on Aug 11, 2008
U.S. Gymnast Jonathan Horton is known for his daredevil moves on the high bar and is the U.S. teams best chance at gold in the all-around competition at the Beijing Olympics. His teammate, 24-year-old Justin Spring, had to overcome a string of injuries to earn a spot on the U.S. team. During a recent interview with VOA's Roger Hsu, The two Olympic athletes discussed their individual paths to the Beijing Games.
