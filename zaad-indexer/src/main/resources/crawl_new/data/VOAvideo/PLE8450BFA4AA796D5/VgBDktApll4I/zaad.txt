Rodeo Clowns Fight Bulls
Uploaded on Apr 6, 2009
One of America's most popular homegrown sports is the rodeo, where cowboys pit their skill and endurance against bucking broncos and bulls. It's dangerous work, and injuries among bull riders, in particular, are common. But they would be in greater danger without the clowns, also called bull fighters, who distract the beasts and allow the riders to escape. VOA's Greg Flakus went to the world's biggest rodeo in Houston and filed this report about the men who spar with bulls.
