International Youth Camp Fosters Olympic Spirit
Uploaded on Aug 11, 2008
Nearly 500 youngsters from around the world will get a close-up view of the Olympics at the Olympic Youth Camp, which got underway in Beijing Wednesday. The program was started at the Stockholm Olympics in 1912 and has been a regular part of the Olympiad since the Tokyo games in 1964. Mike O'Sullivan reports, the camp uses sports to build bridges between cultures.
