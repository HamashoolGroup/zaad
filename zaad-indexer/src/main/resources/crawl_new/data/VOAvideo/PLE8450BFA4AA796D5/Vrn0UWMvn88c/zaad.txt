Confederations Cup Football Matches Begin Soon
Uploaded on Jun 4, 2009
The football, or soccer, world's seven regional champions are coming to South Africa for the Confederations Cup, often called the mini-World Cup. The two weeks of top-level matches are seen as a test run for next year's World Cup and preparations in South Africa are in high gear. VOA's Scott Bobb reports from a major tourism convention in Durban.
