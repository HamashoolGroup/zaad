US Soccer Team Prepares to Face World Champion Italy
Uploaded on Jun 15, 2009
The U.S. men's soccer team is in South Africa where it will play its opening match of the eight-team Confederations Cup on Monday in Pretoria against World Champion Italy. This will be followed by group games against five-time World Cup champion Brazil and Egypt. European champion Spain, Iraq, New Zealand and host South Africa are in the other group for first round play. VOA Sports Editor Parke Brewer reports from Pretoria.
