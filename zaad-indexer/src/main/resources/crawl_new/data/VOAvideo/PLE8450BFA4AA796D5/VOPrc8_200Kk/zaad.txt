Ghana Seeks to Give Fans, All of Africa, More Reason to Celebrate
Uploaded on Jul 1, 2010
World Cup fever is building across Africa as Ghana prepares to play Uruguay Friday in Johannesburg's Soccer City stadium. Ghana qualified for the quarterfinals for the first time by beating the United States on Monday. Its fans are hoping the Black Stars will win again and become the first African team to qualify for a World Cup semifinal. VOA's Scott Bobb reports from Johannesburg.
