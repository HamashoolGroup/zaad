Olympic Athletes Promote Sports with 'Right to Play'
Uploaded on Mar 1, 2010
As the world focuses on the Winter Olympics in Vancouver, Canada, an athlete-based, international humanitarian organization is using the spotlight to help promote sports in the poorest parts of the planet. The organization, called Right to Play, helps more than one million children around the world. VOA's Kane Farabaugh has more.
