China Throws Its Biggest Party Ever
Uploaded on Aug 11, 2008
The moment has finally arrived. The 29th [XXIX] Summer Olympiad in Beijing, China has begun. Even before Beijing was selected to host the Olympics Games seven years ago, China has been planning for this event. The country spent over one billion dollars building stadiums and is sending its largest team ever, 639 athletes, to compete. Expectations for the athletes and the country are high. And as VOA Stephanie Ho in Beijing reports the entire city is caught up in Olympic fever.
