London Begins Two-Year Countdown to 2012 Olympics
Uploaded on Jul 28, 2010
Sports stars and politicians have arrived in London to tour the Olympic Park construction site, where in exactly two years, athletes from around the world will compete in the 2012 Games. Authorities in Britain say they are ahead of schedule and the International Olympic Committee has indicated its satisfaction with London's progress. But, there are concerns over the cost of the Games following the global recession and widespread government spending cuts.
