Historic Yankee Stadium Hosts Its Last Game
Uploaded on Sep 23, 2008
It was the end of an era in New York as legendary Yankee Stadium hosted its final baseball game on Sunday. The stadium was home to the New York Yankees for 85 years and hosted many iconic moments in baseball history. VOA's Barry Unger takes a look at the stadium's remarkable history. Jim Bertel narrates.
