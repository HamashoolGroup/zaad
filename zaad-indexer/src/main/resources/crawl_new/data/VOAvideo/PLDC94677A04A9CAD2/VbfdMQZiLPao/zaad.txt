Obama Pledges US Aid for Ebola Recovery Efforts
Published on Apr 15, 2015
President Barack Obama is pledging more U.S. assistance to nations of West Africa as they recover from the devastating Ebola epidemic. The pledge came Wednesday, when Obama hosted the leaders of Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone at the White House. VOA White House Correspondent Luis Ramirez reports.
