Longer Lives for Type 1 Diabetics Who Strictly Control Blood Sugar
Published on Jan 12, 2015
People with type 1 diabetes are more likely to have an early death and suffer severe long-term complications than the rest of the population. A new study looks at whether maintaining tighter control of blood sugar levels over time can help lower these risks. More from VOA's Carol Pearson.
