IMF Outlook: Rich Nations To Grow Faster in 2015
Published on Apr 14, 2015
The International Monetary Fund says the global economy will grow a tiny bit faster in 2015. Growth in emerging nations will slow down, it says, while advanced nations will expand a little more quickly. It?s a forecast that reflects the lingering effects of the financial crisis, falling oil prices and fluctuating currency exchange rates. VOA's Mil Arcega reports.
