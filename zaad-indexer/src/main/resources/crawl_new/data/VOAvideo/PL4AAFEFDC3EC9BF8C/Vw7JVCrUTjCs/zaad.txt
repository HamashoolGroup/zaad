US Economy Grows at Fastest Pace Since 2003
Published on Dec 23, 2014
The U.S. economy is growing faster than first thought. The Commerce Department released its final estimate of third quarter GDP (Gross Domestic Product) Tuesday, showing the economy expanded at a five percent annual pace from July to September. The reason? Consumers are feeling better about the economy. As Mil Arcega reports, that sentiment bodes well for the U.S. economy in 2015.
