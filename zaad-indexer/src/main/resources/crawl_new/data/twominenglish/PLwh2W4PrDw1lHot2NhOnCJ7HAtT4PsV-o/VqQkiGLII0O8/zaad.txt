Baseball is a sport which is pretty popular in America but is rarely played in other countries. You might be new to baseball and probably won't know what a strike is, or a home run, and most likely there are many other terms you just don't know the meaning. It can seem complicated and there are a lot of terms which have a different meaning in baseball than they do somewhere else. In this lesson we will learn some baseball terminology to help you understand the game, and which will also help you enjoy yourself more if you have the opportunity to actually watch a baseball game.
