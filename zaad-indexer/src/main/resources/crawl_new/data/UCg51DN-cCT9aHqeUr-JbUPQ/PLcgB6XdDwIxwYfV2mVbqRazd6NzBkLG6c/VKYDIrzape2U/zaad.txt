6 Minute Vocabulary: Binomials - BBC Learning English
Published on Feb 9, 2016
Mr Christmas said he never gets sick and tired of his celebrations. Sick and tired is a phrase which means 'annoyed and bored'. Phrases like this with two nouns joined by and are very common - and useful - in English. They're called binomials. You can see more examples of binomials in the Session Vocabulary box on the right.
