BBC Learning English - 6 Minute English '14 ? Learn a thousand foreign words
Published on Feb 9, 2016
British people are lazy linguists ? they don't tend to learn foreign languages. A campaign is now encouraging everyone in the UK to learn at least 1,000 words of another language. The 1,000 Words Campaign follows concerns that the country is losing out on international trade and jobs because of poor language skills. Rob and Finn discuss the benefits of learning a new language and look at vocabulary to do with becoming a linguist.
