BBC Learning English - 6 Minute English '14: Downsizing for Italy's Casanovas
Published on Jan 20, 2016
Some Italian men are known as Casanovas. They woo - or chase - women and buy them expensive gifts. But now, because of the economic downturn, some of them are struggling to live up to the reputation they once had. Rob and Finn - non-Casanovas - discuss the cutbacks these men are making and what it means for their mistresses, or their 'secret women'.
