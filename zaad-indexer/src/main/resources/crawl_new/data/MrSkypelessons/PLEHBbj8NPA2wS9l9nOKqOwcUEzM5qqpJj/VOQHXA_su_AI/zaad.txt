This elementary English course is for independent students. This lesson introduces past continuous, and you are advised to answer my questions and speak to your monitors. Please try the following quiz http://www.quiz.biz/quizz-369678.html PAST CONTINUOUS (Use it when you were 'in the middle of' an action) I/he/she was ___ing You/we/they were ____ing ANSWERS She saw a squirrel when she was running While she was walking, she dropped her keys She fell over when she was dancing It started raining when she was cycling She helped her granny when she was cooking While she was buying a cake, her mobile rang She had an idea when she was painting When she was singing a bird landed on her hand Please answer the following questions What were you doing at half past 8, yesterday morning? What were you doing at quarter past 4, yesterday afternoon? What was your family doing when you got home last night? What were you wearing when you went to work yesterday?
