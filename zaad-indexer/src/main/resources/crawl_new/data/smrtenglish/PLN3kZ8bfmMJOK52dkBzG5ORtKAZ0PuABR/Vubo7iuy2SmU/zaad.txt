Ukrainian Maidan Students - Smrt Scolarship for students effected by the Maidan Square uprising
Published on Oct 21, 2014
15 students who participated in the Maidan Square uprising received scholarships to attend an 8 week English course at CCEL summer 2014. Developed in conjunction with OWL Vancouver the program enabled the students to regain their strength, re evaluate their vision and to return to the Ukraine with skills and perspective that would strengthen their ability to achieve their goals and succeed in future study programs and careers.
