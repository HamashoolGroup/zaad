Learn British English Free: Grammar Phone-in 2
Published on Sep 17, 2015
This is part 2 of the BBC Radio 5 Live ?Up All Night? Grammar phone-in from 24 August 2015. Presenter Dotun Adebayo and British grammar experts Neville Gwynne and Terry Victor discuss a number of grammatical issues. In this part, they debate how important it is to learn correct grammar and use it when speaking English; splitting infinitives; prepositional prounouns. Copyright to the BBC. I do not own this audio.
