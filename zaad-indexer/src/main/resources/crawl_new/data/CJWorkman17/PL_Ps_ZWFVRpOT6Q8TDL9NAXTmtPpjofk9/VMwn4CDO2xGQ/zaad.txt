Skype username: BritishEnglish_Teacher Busuu.com username: BritishEnglishTutor Strange jobs Feng Shui consultants: These consultants visit offices and homes to ensure a harmonious environment; Embalmers: The specialists in preparing bodies for the afterlife; Dog psychologists: Individuals trained to analyse the behaviours and characteristics of troublesome canines; Kosher certification consultants: Experts on Judaism who ensure that food is prepared in accordance with Jewish law; Painting authenticators: The teams who see through art fraudsters to differentiate between true versus fake paintings; Saddle consultants: The people who ensure equine enthusiasts have the most comfortable ride possible and that their horses have the perfect riding accessory; The Ravenmaster: England's one and only custodian of the Tower of London's ravens. Derrick Coyle is responsible for the birds' welfare, and ensuring the royal decree issued by Charles II - that there are always six ravens at the Tower - is obeyed. Lego sculptor: And it really does exist. In fact, there are eight Lego 'master model builders' around the world. Pet food taster: The next time you see a job advert for a 'senior food technologist', be wary. It could actually be a post for a dog food taster. What, did you think dogs did all the tasting? Dog Yoga Teacher: Why not start your own local class and make some extra cash?
