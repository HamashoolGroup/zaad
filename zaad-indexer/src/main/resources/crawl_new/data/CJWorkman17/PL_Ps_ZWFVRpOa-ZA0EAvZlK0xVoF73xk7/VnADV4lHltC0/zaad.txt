Business English Use modal verbs, please, thank you; avoid slang and informal language Emails Formal example: Dear Michael, I can confirm I will attend our meeting at 1000 next Thursday. Kind regards, Chris Informal example: Hi Mike, I'll be at the meeting next Thursday. See you later, Chris Meetings Chair (to lead) Minute taker takes minutes COP Flag (verb) Action (noun and verb) Action notes / log Management speak: Push the envelope Touch base Advice for getting a job CV -- two pages Covering letter -- one page http://prospects.ac.uk/cvs_and_cover_... Application -- concise; relevant; examples Interview -- perfect preparation / research company; dress appropriately; be confident when greeting; seem personable (body language / eye contact); back up answers with examples from work experience; always have questions to ask; don't talk about money much Search Internet for advice and tips (e.g. BBC; British Council) Crazy modern interview questions - BBC: http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/business-22...
