Learn British English Free: Four Expressions
Published on Aug 18, 2015
This lesson is about four common British English expressions. Please click "show more" for notes and resources. Bring the weather with you (Normally used when someone?s back from holiday) ?Welcome back, I see you didn?t bring the weather with you!? Already feels like a long time ago (Said by someone who?s recently returned from holiday) ?How was your holiday?? ?It was wonderful, but it already feels so long ago.? Weekends go too quickly ?How was your weekend?? ?Lovely, thanks, but went a bit too quickly.? ?It was very nice but they go so fast, don?t they?? Not bad for a (day of the week) ?Hi, how are you?? ?Not bad for a Monday.? ?Not bad for a Tuesday.?
