Learn British English Free: here's "one" for you
Published on Dec 7, 2015
In this lesson, I explain many (but not all) of the uses in English of ?one?. Please read below. This one / that one (can be specified object or concept / subject / person) ?Can I have that one, please?? (Shop / restaurant) ?I?ll take this one, please.? ?Just this one, please.? Nice one (thanks) Good one (well done) I owe you one / you owe me one (favour) Here?s one for you (depends on context) These videos on my Youtube channel provide free educational material about British English, British pronunciation and British accent training.
