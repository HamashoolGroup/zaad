The reality of the situation is that_________________________________. 2. The bottom line is that____________________________________?____. 3. At the end of the day,____________________________________?____. The are all very similar in meaning. They mean "the most important point", or "In conclusion,___." The video gives several examples, and also elicits examples from the listener, using topics like robots and video games. The video also shows how to combine these idioms with "in terms of", and "when it comes to". Any English student can quickly made big changes in their English communication skills by learning and using these three great idioms. And as always, remember to catch these expressions in real life.
