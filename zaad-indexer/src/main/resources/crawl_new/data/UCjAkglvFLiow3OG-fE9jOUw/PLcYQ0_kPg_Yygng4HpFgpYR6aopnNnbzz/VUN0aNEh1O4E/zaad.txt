--- VIDEO TITLE: Making nouns plural - English Lessons with inlingua Vancouver Usually add 'S': wave - waves, hat - hats, clock - clocks Ends in an 'o' add 'es': hero - heroes, potato - potatoes, tomato - tomatoes S, x, ch, sh add 'es': glass - glasses, church - churches, box - boxes, bush - bushes Vowel + y add 's': play - plays, monkey - monkeys Consonant + y add 'es': party - parties, duty - duties Proper nouns add 's: The Kennedy family - The Kennedys Compound nouns with main noun: one "father-in-law" - two fathers-in-law, one chief of staff - two chiefs of staff without main noun: one trade-in, two trade-ins --- TEACHER: TASH Connect with us!
