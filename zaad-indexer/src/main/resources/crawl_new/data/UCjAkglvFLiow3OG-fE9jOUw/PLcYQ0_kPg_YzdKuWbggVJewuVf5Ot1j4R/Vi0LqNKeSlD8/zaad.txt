How to use it. After a question: This rule is easy, isn't it? Uncertainty / Doubt: The author lived from 1810 (?) to 1895. Polite request: Would you please bring us a chocolate milk? Direct vs Indirect: Is today Monday? (Yes, you add the question mark) I asked whether today is Monday. (No need for "?") Josh asked if we were to the cinema. (No need for "?")
