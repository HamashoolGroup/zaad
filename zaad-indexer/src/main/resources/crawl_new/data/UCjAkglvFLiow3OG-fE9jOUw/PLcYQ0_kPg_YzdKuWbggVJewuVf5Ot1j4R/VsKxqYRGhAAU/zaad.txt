--- VIDEO TITLE: How to split infinitives - English lessons with inlingua Vancouver --- VIDEO DESCRIPTION: How to split infinitives - English lessons with inlingua Vancouver An infinitive is a verb with the word 'to' in front of it. Splitting an infinitive means putting one or more words between 'to' and the verb. Infinitive to run to swim to jump Non-split infinitive to run quickly to swim eagerly to jump determinedly Split infinitive to quickly run to eagerly swim to determinedly jump Weak: It is usually better to NOT split infinitives. Better: It is usually better NOT split infinitives. Right: I want you to study the material for the test. More emphasis: I want you to thoroughly study the material for the test. Confusing: I want you to thoroughly and deliberately, without cutting any corners, making any excuses, study for the test.
