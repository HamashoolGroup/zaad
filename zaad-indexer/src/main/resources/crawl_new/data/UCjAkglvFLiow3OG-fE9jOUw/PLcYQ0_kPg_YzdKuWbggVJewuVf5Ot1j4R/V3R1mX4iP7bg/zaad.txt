--- VIDEO TITLE: Past Perfect - English Lesson with inlingua Vancouver --- VIDEO DESCRIPTION: Past Perfect - We use the past perfect when we are giving time order to TWO situations in the past. The past perfect shows which event happened first. It is formed subject + had + past participle ?I had left the house before the post arrived?.
