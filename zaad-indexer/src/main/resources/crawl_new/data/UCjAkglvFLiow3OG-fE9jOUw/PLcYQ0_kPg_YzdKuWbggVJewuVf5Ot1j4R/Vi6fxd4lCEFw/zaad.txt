--- VIDEO TITLE: Stative Verbs (verb to be) - English Lessons with inlingua Vancouver --- VIDEO DESCRIPTION: Normally we use the verb "to be" to the status or characteristics of something or someone (as a stative verb). It says what I am, what you are and what something is. Present Simple - Am, Is, Are - I am a teacher. Past Simple - Was, Were - I was a student. Future Simple - Will be - I will be a student. --- TEACHER: LINDA Connect with us!
