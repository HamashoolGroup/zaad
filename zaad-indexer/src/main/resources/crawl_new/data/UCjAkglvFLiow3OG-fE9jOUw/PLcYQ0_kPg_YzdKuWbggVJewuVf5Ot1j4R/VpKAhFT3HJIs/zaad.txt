--- VIDEO TITLE: The difference between Hear and Listen - English Lessons with inlingua Vancouver --- VIDEO DESCRIPTION: Hear vs Listen- Hear- to have sound enter your ears without putting in any effort or trying. It has a passive meaning ?Can you hear me?? (am I loud enough to understand) Listen- active meaning, you are trying, you are paying attention. ?Are you listening??
