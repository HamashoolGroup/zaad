---PREVIOUS VIDEOS: Simple Present Tense (Present Simple) - English Lesson with inlingua Vancouver - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oWpaG... --- VIDEO TITLE: Stative Verbs (Simple Present pt.2) - English Lessons with inlingua Vancouver --- VIDEO DESCRIPTION: Simple Present Tense (Present Simple) is the form of the verb which shows the action/state takes place in the present. State Verbs are verbs that describe a state, not an action. Usually refer to conditions which are NOT changing. ** Usually do not take the -ing form. Verbs that show: 1. Thought: Believe, understand, know. Ex: He knows Korean. 2. Possession: Have, own, want. Ex: I have a dog. 3. Senses: Hear, taste, smell, see, touch. 4. Emotions: Like, *love, hate. Ex: I like coffee *Love - Informal -ing. --- TEACHER: Linda Connect with us!
