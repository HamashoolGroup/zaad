--- VIDEO TITLE: The difference between Same and Similar - English Lessons with inlingua Vancouver --- TEACHER: Linda --- VIDEO DESCRIPTION: They are Similar, but they are not the same. Similiar vs Same Same means they are identical with no differences. Same: Touch, look and feel are identical. Example: I have the same Nike shoes as yours. Similar: means they are almost identical but have some nimor differences. They may look the same but different. Example: I wear similar sunglasses as yours. *In this example the sunglasses can be from the same brand or size or colour. Connect with us!
