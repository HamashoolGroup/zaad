They describe a state/condition which have no definite beginning or no definite end (Emotions, senses, thoughts, possession). Exception - Think, Have, See, Taste, Smell and Feel. 1. I have long hair (stative) The students are having a meeting (temporary) - beginning - end 2. The man felt that he was not the right person for the job (stative) Are you feeling well today?
