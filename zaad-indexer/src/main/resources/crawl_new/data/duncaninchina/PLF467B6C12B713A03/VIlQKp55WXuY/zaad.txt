Learning English-Lesson Twenty One (Compliments)
Uploaded on Nov 20, 2008
( NOW IN HIGH QUALITY ) In this lesson we look at giving compliments. It is important to know the difference between compliment and complement. They are pronounced the same, but mean different things. Compliment means 'admire and praise someone for something'. Complement means 'add to something in a way that enhances or improves'.
