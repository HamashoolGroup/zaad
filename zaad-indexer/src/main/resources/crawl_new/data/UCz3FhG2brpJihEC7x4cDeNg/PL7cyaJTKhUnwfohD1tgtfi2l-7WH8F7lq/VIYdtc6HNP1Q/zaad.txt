TOEFL Writing Skills Preparation 1: Overview of the TOEFL iBT Independent Writing Task
Published on Mar 11, 2013
In the first video of the series, we provide an overview of the TOEFL iBT Writing Section, specifically focusing on the INDEPENDENT WRITING TASK. We also begin to explore how to prepare for the task by examining the different topics that can be found on the test, discussing how to think about the test topics more deeply, and by exploring the importance of logic in our arguments.
