This is the fifth video in a series to teach rooms in the home and the objects inside them. Many parents of children with Autism and learning disabilities have told us how the repetition of this series helped their child learn to communicate better. This video introduces the things in the kitchen. Builds on the line of questioning used in every room in the home - "What's this?" "It's a..." "Where is the..." "The ... is in the ..."
