Lesson 14 - Weather, Numbers, Time & The Calendar How is the weather? (How's the weather?) It is sunny. (It's sunny.) Cardinal numbers: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5... Ordinal numbers: 1st, 2nd, 3rd... Fractions: 1/2, 1/3, 1/4, 2/3... Time: 1:00 = 1 o'clock 1:15 = a quarter past one 1:30 = half past one 1:45 = quarter to two I play baseball AT 2:00. The Calendar: Year, month, week, day Months of the Year: What month is it? January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December My birthday is IN September. My birthday is ON September 21st. Days of the Week: What day is it. What's the date? Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday Prepositions of time: It's rainy ON Monday. I swim ON Tuesday. I wake up AT 7:00.
