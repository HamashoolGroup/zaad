The first, most HORRIBLE thing you could do for your learning of English is to try to master grammar using a grammar textbook. There are two things you need to remember about learning English grammar: The first is that you should learn grammar naturally. The second is that you do not need to know the names of grammar points. If you go ask a native speaker what a past participle is, or what a split infinitive is, they will not be able to tell you. Most native speakers do NOT know this information. Only an English teacher would be able to tell you what either of these two things are. And this should give you some really important clue to how to learn English naturally.
