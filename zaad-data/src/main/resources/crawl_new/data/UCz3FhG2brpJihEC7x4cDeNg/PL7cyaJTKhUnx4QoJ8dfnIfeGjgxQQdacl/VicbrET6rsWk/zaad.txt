Learning the 44 Sounds of English for Beginning Students: Video 9-Diphthongs
Published on Jun 17, 2015
In this video we learn about DIPHTHONGS, a type of VOWEL that uses two movements of the mouth to make the sound. There are 3 DIPHTHONGS in English: the /OY/ sound, the /OW/ sound, and the LONG VOWEL /I/ sound. This is video 9 of the series.
