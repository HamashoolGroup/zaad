Iowa Man Owns 12,000 Golf Balls
Published on May 24, 2012
An Iowa man who started collecting golf balls as a kid says he now has more than 12,000 of them in various rooms of his homes. He finds most of them on a local golf course, cleans them up and sells them on his front yard.
