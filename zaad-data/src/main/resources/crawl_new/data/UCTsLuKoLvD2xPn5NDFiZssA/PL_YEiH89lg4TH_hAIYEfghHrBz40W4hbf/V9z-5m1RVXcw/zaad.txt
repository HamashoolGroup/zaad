Business English: Stand out in your interview
Published on Sep 14, 2014
If there are a lot of excellent candidates applying for the same job as you, you really need to stand out in your interview. The best way to do that is by preparing 3 or 4 key messages you would like to communicate in the interview. You can communicate these messages through stories. This business English video tip shows you how you can structure the stories that will help you stand out in your interview.
