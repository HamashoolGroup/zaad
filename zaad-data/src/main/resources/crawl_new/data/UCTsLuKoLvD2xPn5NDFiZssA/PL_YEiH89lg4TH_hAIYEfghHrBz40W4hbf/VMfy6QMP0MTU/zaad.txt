The elevator pitch. Business English lesson with Rachel. Kostenlos Englisch lernen.
Published on Jan 23, 2014
You only have 30 seconds to introduce yourself to a potential client, employer or investor. And you want to get as much information as possible in your 30 seconds. Rachel gives you a simple formula for a 30 second introduction. This is also called the elevator pitch. In international business people often have little time to listen to you. So, make sure you make an impression. Watch the video and find out how you can do this.
