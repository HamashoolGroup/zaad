Giving feedback is a key skill for managers and team leaders. It is also useful for anybody working in a team. When English is not your first language, it might be difficult to remember a process for giving feedback and the correct English vocabulary and grammar. In this video, I talk about a process for giving feedback and useful phrases for each stage of the process. Hope you enjoy it and find it useful.
