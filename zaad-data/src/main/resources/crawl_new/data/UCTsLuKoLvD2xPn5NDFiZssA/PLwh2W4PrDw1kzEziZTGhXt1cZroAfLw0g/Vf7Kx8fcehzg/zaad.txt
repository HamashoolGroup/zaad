Cancelling an Appointment - Business English Lessons - Spoken English Videos
Published on Nov 17, 2013
There are times when you may not be able to keep an appointment. In this case it's very important to inform the person you were supposed to meet so that they don't end up waiting for you. You should inform people of a cancelled appointment as early as possible. In this lesson we will see some words and phrases that you can use when you want to cancel an appointment.
