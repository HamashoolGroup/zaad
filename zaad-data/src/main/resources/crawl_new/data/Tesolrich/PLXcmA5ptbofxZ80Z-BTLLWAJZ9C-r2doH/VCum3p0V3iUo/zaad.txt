Published on Oct 13, 2013
Understanding the different types of mistakes you will make when you are learning English is important, also when you are improving and moving to higher English levels. In this video I talk about the different types and how English teachers define them as 'impeding errors or mistakes' or 'non-impeding errors or mistake'. The mistakes when speaking and writing English can be: pronunciation grammar sentence construction word order vocabulary I also suggest different learning techniques to help different types of mistakes, depending on your short term and long term English goals.
