Hickory Dickory Dock Nursery Rhyme for Kids
Uploaded on Dec 27, 2011
Hickory Dickory Dock, sing along with me and do the actions in this classic nursery rhyme. Purchase this video for offline viewing here: http://www.dreamenglish.com/videosales Teaching Tips: Watch the video once. Next, replay the video and pause at certain points. Ask your children or students questions like, ""What time is it now?", " Is the mouse going up or down?" The song can be used as an introduction to time, as well as directions. Have fun!
