Kids Fun Rhyme 1: Animals
Uploaded on Nov 21, 2011
Try do the this fun rhyme with me! Here are the words: Elephant, elephant Pop, pop, pop Tiger, tiger Go, stop Monkey, Monkey Drop, drop, drop Lion, lion, Up, up, up Elephant, Monkey, Tiger, Lion Pop, pop, pop, pop, stop! Can you do this Rhyme? Post a video response and share! Teaching Tips: Pause the video at the beginning and teach the names of the animals. Then after watching the video, pause it at the end and ask your child or student if they can say the animal names as your point to them! Rhyme by Matt R. Copyright 2011 http://www.dreamenglish.com
