Is English Illogical? Think Again!
Uploaded on Feb 2, 2012
Do you think of English as illogical and full of exceptions? This video challenges you to rethink that assumption and see that English is based on a logical code which can be easily understood. Learning all the rules that govern English spelling, rather than just a few commonly taught rules that account for less than 50% of English words, paves the way for successful reading and spelling for all learners. For more information, see "Uncovering the Logic of English: A Common-sense Solution to America's Literacy Crisis" by Denise Eide, available at http://www.LogicOfEnglish.com
