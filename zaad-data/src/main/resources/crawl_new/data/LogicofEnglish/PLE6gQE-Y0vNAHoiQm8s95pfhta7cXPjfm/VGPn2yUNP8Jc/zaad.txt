Spelling Dictation: A Multi-Sensory Approach to Reading and Spelling by the Logic of English
Published on Jul 31, 2012
Learn how to dictate words using the Logic of English style of spelling dictation. This presentation by Denise Eide will review the basic approach, demonstrate how to mark words, introduce finger spelling, and explain how this method benefits all learning styles. Spelling Dictation, also called spelling analysis, is an integral component in helping students master the phonograms and spelling rules and develop the decoding skills they need for success in reading.
