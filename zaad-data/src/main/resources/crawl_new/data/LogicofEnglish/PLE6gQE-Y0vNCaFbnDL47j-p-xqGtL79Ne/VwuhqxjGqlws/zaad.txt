Phonograms and Spelling Rules Part 2, Continued: I and Y, Adding Suffixes. Section 7.9 introduces Spelling Rules 5, 6, and 7 which govern the sounds of I and Y. Denise teaches the spelling rules for adding suffixes to any base word, using the Adding Suffixes Flow Chart on page 158 of the Teacher Training Manual. Accented syllables are important for adding suffixes to words ending in one vowel followed by one consonant.
