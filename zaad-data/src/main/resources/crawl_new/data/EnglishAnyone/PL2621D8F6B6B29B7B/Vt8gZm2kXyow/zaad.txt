Lesson 15 - Past Simple & Future Simple Tenses Past Simple (negative) To be: I was (not). - I wasn't. You were (not) - You weren't. He/she/it was (not) - He/she/it wasn't. We/they were (not) - We/they weren't. Future Simple (negative) To be: I/you/he/she/it/we/they will (not) be. - won't be. I'll/you'll/he'll/she'll/it'll/we'll/the?y'll (not) be. Past Simple (negative) To do: I/you/he/she/it/we/they did (not) do. didn't do. Future Simple (negative) To do: I/you/he/she/it/we/they will (not) do. - won't do. I'll/you'll/he'll/she'll/it'll/we'll/the?y'll (not) do. Past Simple Regular Verb Rules: 1: verb + ed (walk-walked) 2: last letter e + d (shave-shaved) 3: vowel+y + ed (play-played) 4: consonant+y + ies (cry-cries) 5: one syllable words: short vowel+consonant + double consonant+ed (stop-stopped) (NOT w/x mix-mixed) 6: for two syllable verbs with final syllable stressed: short vowel+consonant + double consonant+ed (admit-admitted) 7: for two syllable verbs with final syllable NOT stressed: short vowel+consonant + ed (happen-happened) did (not) + verb. - didn't + verb. Future Simple Regular Verbs: I/you/he/she/it/we/they will (not) + verb. - won't + verb. I'll/you'll/he'll/she'll/it'll/we'll/the?y'll (not) + verb. Past Simple Irregular Verbs: I/you/he/she/it/we/they past tense of verb did (not) + verb. - didn't + verb. Future Simple Irregular Verbs: I/you/he/she/it/we/they will (not) + verb. - won't + verb. I'll/you'll/he'll/she'll/it'll/we'll/the?y'll (not) + verb.
