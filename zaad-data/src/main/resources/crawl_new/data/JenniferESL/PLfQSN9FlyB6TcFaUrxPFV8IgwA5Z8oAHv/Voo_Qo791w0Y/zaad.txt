This is a lesson in four parts. Part 1 - "Awake and Asleep" (a short story with reduced clauses). Review what adverb clauses are and why phrases are different. Part 2 - Learn how to reduce adverb clauses of time. Part 3 - Learn how to reduce adverb clauses of reason, how to make the phrases negative, and how to work with perfect and passive verbs. Part 4 - Two review exercises.
