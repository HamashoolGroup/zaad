An elementary English lesson which practises past simple (regular and irregular) verb forms, and revises the topic of time. Please speak in this course (on your own, if you don't have a partner). Pause the video and ask/answer questions using the pictures as prompts. Then listen to check for mistakes. https://www.skype-lessons.com/element... Please answer the following questions What time did you wake up, this morning? What did you eat for breakfast? What time did you start work? What did you eat for lunch? What time did you finish work? What time did you go to bed last night?
