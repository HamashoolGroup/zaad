Best Wishes, Teacher Frank VOCABULARY FOCUS 1 GOOD TASTE - to have a fine choice in liking things (i.e. ideas, art, food, clothes, people etc.) 2 THE ONE - the one major love of a person's life 3 UP FRONT - (adv) first, at the beginning 4 JUST LIKE THAT - refers to an action done suddenly and unexpectedly 5 UPPITY - (adj) acting as if of a higher class or status that one really is 6 SKANK - (n) a corrupt or unpleasant person 7 PERFECTLY ADEQUATE - completely average or ordinary 8 ANAL - (adj) (psychology) implies that a person may be fussy or over organized because their childhood toilet or potty training was incomplete or interfered with (not polite) 9 WORK OUT - to go well or ideally 10 GET OVER - to mentally overcome a difficulty; to resolve an inner conflict 11 SID AND NANCY - a reference to Sid Vicious, a musician in a punk rock band, and Nancy Spungen.
