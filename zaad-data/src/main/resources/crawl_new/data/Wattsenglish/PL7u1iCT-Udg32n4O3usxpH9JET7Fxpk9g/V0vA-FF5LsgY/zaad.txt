Teacher Training: Yummy, what is in my tummy, sing and dance along
Published on Feb 24, 2016
This original video from a Teacher Training in Prague shows how much fun you can have while both teaching and learning. New trainees from various kindergartens, elementary schools, Czech Republic and Slovakia sing a song about Food together with the native English speaking teachers, having fun with their possible students and teaching them ?What is in my tummy?? Teachers can use flashcards or real objects.
