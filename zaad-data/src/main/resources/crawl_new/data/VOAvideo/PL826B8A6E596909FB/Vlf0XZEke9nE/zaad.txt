Egypt Releases Jailed Journalist as Relations With Qatar Thaw
Published on Feb 3, 2015
The release of Al Jazeera journalist Peter Greste from a jail in Cairo has been greeted as a small step forward for basic rights in Egypt. But two of his colleagues remain behind bars. The journalists are being seen as pawns in a power game between Egypt and Qatar, centered on the role of the Muslim Brotherhood.
