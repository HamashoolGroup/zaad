Rights Group: International Community Not Taking in Syrian Refugees
Published on Dec 6, 2014
The human rights group Amnesty International is calling on the international community to do more to address the Syrian refugee crisis. Millions of Syrians have fled over the borders, but only a small fraction have been given asylum in countries outside the region. The United Nations hosts a conference on the issue Tuesday.
