Yemen in Turmoil, Emergency Parliament Session Planned
Published on Jan 24, 2015
Yemen's parliament is scheduled to hold an emergency session Sunday following the resignation of the president and the government. Armed Houthi rebels stormed the capital, Sanaa, earlier this week, plunging the country into further turmoil and triggering protests in the south. VOA's Henry Ridgwell reports.
