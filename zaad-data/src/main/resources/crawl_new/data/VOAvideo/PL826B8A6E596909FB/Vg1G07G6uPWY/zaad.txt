UN Report Accuses Islamic State of 'Massive Scale' War Crimes
Published on Nov 14, 2014
U.N. investigators have found Islamic State commanders responsible for war crimes and crimes against humanity on a "massive scale" during their drive to capture territory in northeast Syria. A new report released Friday in Geneva says the commanders acted willfully to terrorize populations with public beheadings and stonings. VOA's Zlatica Hoke reports.
