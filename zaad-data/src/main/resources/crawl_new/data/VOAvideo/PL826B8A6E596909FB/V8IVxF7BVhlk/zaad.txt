Britain Hosts Emir Of Qatar Amid Accusations Of Terror Funding
Published on Oct 28, 2014
The Emir of Qatar will visit Britain this week amid accusations that his country has financed extremist Islamist groups in Syria. A number of British lawmakers recently questioned London?s close relationship with Doha. Qatari officials deny any links with extremist groups and say they are working with the West to support moderate opposition groups in Syria. Henry Ridgwell reports from London.
