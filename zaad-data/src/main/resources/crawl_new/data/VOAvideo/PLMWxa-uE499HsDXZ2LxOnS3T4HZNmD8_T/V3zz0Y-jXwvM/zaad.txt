US to Review Refugee Numbers, Resettlement Process
Published on Sep 17, 2015
The Obama administration would like to further increase the number of Syrian refugees that it resettles in the United States, says the State Department. But U.S. officials are balancing the desire to resettle more Syrians with the need to conduct thorough background checks on those considered for admission.
