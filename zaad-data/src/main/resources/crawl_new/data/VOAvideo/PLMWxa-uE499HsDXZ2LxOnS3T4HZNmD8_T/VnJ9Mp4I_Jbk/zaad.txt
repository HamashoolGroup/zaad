Europe Braces for New Wave of Refugees
Published on Sep 7, 2015
Thousands of refugees have been crossing the border from Hungary into Austria, after the Hungarian government started relaxing restrictions. Aid workers and Austrian officials are expecting the influx to grow in the coming days, as word filters back to others that the passage has become easier.
