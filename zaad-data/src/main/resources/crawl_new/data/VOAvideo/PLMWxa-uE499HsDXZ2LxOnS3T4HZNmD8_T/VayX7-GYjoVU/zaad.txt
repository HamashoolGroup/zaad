Hungarian Police Struggle to Control Refugee Influx at Serbian Border
Published on Sep 7, 2015
The influx of refugees from Syria, Iraq, and Afghanistan to Europe is showing no signs of slowing. Aid workers and Hungarian officials say at least another 2,000 people crossed through a gap in the fence between Serbia and Hungary.
