Proposed Pollution Rules Rattle US Power Industry
Published on Nov 19, 2013
The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) just wrapped up a nationwide tour to solicit comments as it develops rules to impose stricter clean air standards on coal-fired power plants, which account for one-third of the nation's climate changing emissions. VOA's Rosanne Skirble reports from the final listening session in Washington.
