'Last Call' Sounds Alarm on Vanishing Water
Published on May 22, 2012
Environmental activist Erin Brockovich sounds the alarm on the world's water crisis in the documentary "Last Call at the Oasis," by Oscar-winning director Jessica Yu. The film highlights water pollution, depletion and potential wars over vanishing water resources. VOA's Penelope Poulou reports.
