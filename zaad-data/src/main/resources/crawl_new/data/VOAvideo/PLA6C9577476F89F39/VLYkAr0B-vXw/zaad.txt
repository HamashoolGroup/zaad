World Bank Calls for Soot, Methane Reduction
Published on Nov 15, 2013
Officials from 192 countries meeting in Warsaw, Poland this week are taking steps to craft a United Nations treaty to replace the Kyoto Protocol, the global climate agreement that expired last year. While talks are expected to lead to a new treaty by 2015, a new World Bank report advocates action now. As Rosanne Skirble reports, it calls for the reduction of shorter-lived pollutants -- as a way to slow the pace of climate change and save lives.
