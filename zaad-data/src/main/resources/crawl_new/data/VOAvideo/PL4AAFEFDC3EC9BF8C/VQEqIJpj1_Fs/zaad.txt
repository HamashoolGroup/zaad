US Job Growth Now Is Strongest Since 1999
Published on Dec 5, 2014
The U.S. job market showed a big improvement in November -- adding 321,000 jobs last month. Although the unemployment rate remained unchanged from the previous month at 5.8 percent, the latest job numbers for November represents the strongest monthly job totals in nearly three years and the best year of employment growth since 1999.
