Mumbai Baker Has Success With Macaroons With Indian Twist
Published on Apr 14, 2015
The French delicacies known as macaroons - sweet, creamy, delicious - are almost impossible to resist. Indian chef and business owner Pooja Dhingra has brought the sugary treat to her hometown. Her bakery -- Le15 Patisserie -- offers traditionally flavored macaroons like vanilla and chocolate, and others with Indian flavors such as masala chai, paan and chilli.
