US Seeks More Tourists from China
Published on Oct 28, 2014
Tourists make more than one billion international visits annually, and the United Nations says their spending gives a boost to the global economy. As VOA's Jim Randle reports, the United States, which had 1.8 million Chinese visitors last year, is stepping up efforts to attract even more.
