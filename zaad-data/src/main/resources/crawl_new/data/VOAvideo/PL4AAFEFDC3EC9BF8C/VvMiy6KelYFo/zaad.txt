US Retailers Upbeat About Holiday Sales
Published on Nov 20, 2014
American retailers are gearing up for the all-important holiday shopping season, when many of them make the bulk of their profits for the year. A modest increase in sales compared with last year is projected, and several economic factors are contributing to the upbeat mood. VOA's Mil Arcega reports.
