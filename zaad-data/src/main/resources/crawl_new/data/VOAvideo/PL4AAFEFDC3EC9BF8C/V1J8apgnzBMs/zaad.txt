Moscow Technology Park Attracting Western Companies Despite Economy, Sanctions
Published on Apr 6, 2015
Russian authorities are describing the country's economic downturn and Western sanctions against it as opportunities to reduce its reliance on energy exports by developing domestic, innovative industries. To spur the growth of high-tech industries, one technology park developed by Moscow city authorities is attracting Western companies that have their eyes on the Russian market.
