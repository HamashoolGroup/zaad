Georgian Wines Make Forbes List of 'Best to Drink in 2015'
Published on Jan 23, 2015
Forbes Magazine has named Georgian wines as one of the "nine best wines and spirits to start drinking in 2015" ? a list that also includes several fine liqueurs and a number of other wine varieties. A number of Washington-area liquor stores and restaurants say Georgian wines are being well-received by consumers in the U.S. capital. VOA's Maia Kay reports.
