Ethiopian Workers Struggle After Repatriation from Saudi Arabia
Published on Oct 8, 2014
More than 160,000 Ethiopians working in Saudi Arabia were expelled last year and sent back home due to new stricter labor regulations in the kingdom. Marthe van der Wolf reports for VOA from Addis Ababa that many of those forcibly repatriated are doing their best to adjust, but are having difficulty making ends meet.
