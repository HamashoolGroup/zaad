US Hiring Rebounds in April; Unemployment Dips to 5.4%
Published on May 8, 2015
The Labor Department says the U.S. economy added 223,000 jobs in April, and the unemployment rate dipped to 5.4 percent, the lowest level in nearly seven years. the job gains were broad-based, and wages increased.
