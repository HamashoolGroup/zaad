Parade of Boats Glitter With Festive Holiday Lights
Published on Dec 17, 2014

A holiday tradition for 32 years, illuminated boats decorated with winter holiday themes, circle the harbor. VOA?s Deborah Block took in the holiday cheer on board one of the boats.
