Imams Get Out the Vote in Afghanistan
Uploaded on Aug 20, 2009
The people of Afghanistan vote Thursday in presidential and provincial elections. Throughout the country, which is officially known as the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan, leaders of the Muslim clergy -- imams -- are doing their part to urge people to vote. Rahim Gul Sarwan went to Nangarhar Province to see how the get-out-the-vote campaign was going.
