Study: Children With Head Lice Should Attend School
Uploaded on Aug 10, 2010
Most children in the northern hemisphere will go back to school at the end of August. Like every year, sales of products to get rid of head lice will soar. Head lice are second only to the common cold among communicable diseases or conditions that affect school children, says the Mayo Clinic. VOA's Vidushi Sinha reports on a new set of guidelines on managing these pests, issued by the American Academy of Pediatrics.
