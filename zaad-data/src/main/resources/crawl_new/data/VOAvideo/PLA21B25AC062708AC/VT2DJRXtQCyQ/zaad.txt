Religious Tension in Malaysia - Linked to Economy, Politics?
Uploaded on Feb 26, 2010
Recently a series of attacks on Christian churches and a Sikh temple in Malaysia focused world attention on ethnic tension in the country. The government, which has been running a "1Malaysia" campaign stressing racial and religious harmony, condemned the attacks. But as VOA's Brian Padden reports, some critics say it may also be contributing to religious and racial divisions to solidify its political base.
