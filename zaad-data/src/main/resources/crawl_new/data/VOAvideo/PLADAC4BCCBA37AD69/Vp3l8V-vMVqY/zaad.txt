Trial Continues in Afghan Atrocities Case
Uploaded on Nov 3, 2011
Testimony continues in the military trial, court martial, of American soldier Calvin Gibbs, accused of masterminding war crimes allegedly committed by the platoon he led in Afghanistan. If convicted, Gibbs could face life in prison without parole. VOA Pentagon correspondent Luis Ramirez reports from Joint Base Lewis-McChord in the U.S. State of Washington.
