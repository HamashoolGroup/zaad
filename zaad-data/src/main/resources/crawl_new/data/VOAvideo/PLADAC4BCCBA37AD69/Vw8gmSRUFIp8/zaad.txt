Presidential Oil Spill Commission Hears Calls for Stronger Response Plan
Uploaded on Sep 29, 2010
A presidential commission held hearings this week in Washington on the events surrounding the BP oil spill in the Gulf of Mexico in April. The National Commission on the BP Deepwater Horizon Oil Spill and Offshore Drilling heard from U.S. Coast Guard officials, Gulf region scientists and a top BP executive. VOA's Laurel Bowman has the story.
