Clinton Global Initiative Garners $63 Billion
Uploaded on Sep 24, 2010
The Clinton Global Initiative has concluded its annual meeting in New York with multi-billion dollar commitments from participants to help address problems of poverty, disease and injustice around the world. VOA Correspondent Peter Fedynsky reports the CGI seeks to raise not only money, but social awareness around the world.
