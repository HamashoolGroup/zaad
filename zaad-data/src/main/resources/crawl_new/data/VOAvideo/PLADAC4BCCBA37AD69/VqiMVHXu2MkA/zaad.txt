G20 Leaders Make Little Progress on Major Differences
Uploaded on Nov 12, 2010
The leaders of the world's top 20 economies have agreed to strive for currency exchange rates that are determined by market forces. And they plan to create a system to identify economic imbalances that threaten economic stability. But as VOA Correspondent Steve Herman reports from the Group of 20 summit in Seoul, their statement was vague on specifics, and did not clearly resolve differences
