Zimbabweans Seek Work Permits in South Africa, Face Deportation
Uploaded on Nov 3, 2010
Thousands of Zimbabweans living in South Africa are besieging government offices seeking work permits under a new program announced in September. VOA's Scott Bobb reports from Johannesburg the South African government has given the estimated one-million undocumented Zimbabweans in the country until the end of December to legalize their status or face deportation.
