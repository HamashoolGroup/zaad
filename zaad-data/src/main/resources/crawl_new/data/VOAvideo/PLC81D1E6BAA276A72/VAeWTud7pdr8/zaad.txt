Doctors Debate Benefits, Risks of Virtual Colonoscopy
Uploaded on Apr 7, 2010
Scientists at the U.S. Food and Drug Administration disagree about the risks from CT scans given during virtual colonoscopies. Doctors who favor the procedure say it is less invasive and more cost effective than a conventional colonoscopy. Those against it say the CT scans expose patients to too much radiation. VOA's Vidushi Sinha has more in this report narrated by Carol Pearson.
