Health, Community Leaders in San Francisco Campaign Against Hepatitis B
Uploaded on Jul 26, 2010
Public health officials in San Francisco, and leaders from its large Asian community, are mounting an aggressive campaign to eradicate hepatitis B from the city. They say demographics are largely responsible for San Francisco having the highest concentration of hepatitis B in the U.S, as well as the highest rates of liver cancer, which is usually caused by the virus. Rachel Silverman reports from San Francisco.
