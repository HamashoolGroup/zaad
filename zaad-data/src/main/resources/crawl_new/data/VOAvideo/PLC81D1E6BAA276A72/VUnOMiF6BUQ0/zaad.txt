After 'King's Speech,' New Interest in Help for Stutterers
Uploaded on Apr 11, 2011
Many people who stutter cheered the recent attention given their condition when Hollywood bestowed four Academy Awards onThe King's Speech , a movie about a real-life British king's struggle to overcome his severe stutter. Now new research is getting to the root causes of stuttering, and a group of young people are learning to cope with this speech disorder.
