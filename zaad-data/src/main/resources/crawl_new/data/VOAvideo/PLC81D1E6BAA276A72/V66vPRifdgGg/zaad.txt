Counterfeit Medicines Grow as Global Threat
Uploaded on Jun 8, 2010
Last month, representatives from all over the world discussed the dangers posed by counterfeit medicines at the World Health Assembly in Geneva. The problem is growing. The World Health Organization [WHO] warns that more than half of all medicines bought online are counterfeit. U.S. experts say there is no one solution. VOA's Vidushi Sinha reports on the severity of this global problem.
