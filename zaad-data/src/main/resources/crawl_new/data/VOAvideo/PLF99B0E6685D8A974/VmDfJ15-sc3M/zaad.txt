Uganda, Colombia Find Alternative to Plastic Waste
Published on Nov 10, 2014
People around the world use disposable plastic containers for water, food and other content because of their practicality and light weight. But if not discarded properly, these plastics can litter populated areas, pollute water systems and create environmental damage. Plastic waste is a real problem in many poor countries.
