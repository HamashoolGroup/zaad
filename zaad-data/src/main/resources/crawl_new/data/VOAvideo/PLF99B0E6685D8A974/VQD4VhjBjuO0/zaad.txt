In Kenya, Family Planning is an Economic Safeguard
Published on Dec 30, 2014
With Kenya's population expected to double by the year 2050, the government and non-governmental organizations are investing heavily in family planning. As VOA East Africa Correspondent Gabe Joselow reports, providing contraception and education to women is not just about population control, it's also about protecting the country's economic interests.
