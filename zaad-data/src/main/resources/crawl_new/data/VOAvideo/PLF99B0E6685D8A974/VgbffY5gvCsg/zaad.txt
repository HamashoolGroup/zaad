Physicians Call on African Leaders to Step Up in Fight Against Ebola
Published on Oct 23, 2014
While the Ebola outbreaks in Senegal and Nigeria were recently declared over, transmission of the disease remains persistent and widespread in the three most affected countries - Guinea, Liberia and Sierra Leone. Health experts from countries that have dealt successfully with past Ebola outbreaks tell VOA's Mariama Diallo that the leaders of those currently-affected nations must switch their focus in order to start seeing some positive changes.
