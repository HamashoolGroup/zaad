Water Packs in Kenya Help Eliminate Waterborne Diseases
Published on Jan 8, 2015
An estimated 783 million people around the world do not have access to clean, running water. But in Kenya, the growing use of water packs is providing safe drinking water to more people while reducing the number of deadly waterborne diseases. Reporter Lenny Ruvaga has details from Nairobi.
