Obama Administration Files Lawsuit Against Arizona Immigration Law
Uploaded on Jul 8, 2010
The fierce debate in the United States over illegal immigration has reached a higher level. Earlier this week (Tuesday, July 6th), the Justice Department filed a lawsuit challenging the constitutionality of (the western state of) Arizona's new law that targets individuals suspected of being in the country illegally. VOA's Carolyn Presutti takes a look at the legal controversy and how this could affect future immigration efforts.
