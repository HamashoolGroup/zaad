Six Grassroots Activists Win $150,000 Goldman Environmental Prize
Uploaded on Apr 29, 2010
Every April during the observances surrounding Earth Day, grassroots environmental activists from six regions of the world are honored with the Goldman Environmental Prize. As VOA'S Rosanne Skirble reports, the privately-funded, $150,000 awards recognize individuals who've shown courage and initiative in responding to environmental problems.
