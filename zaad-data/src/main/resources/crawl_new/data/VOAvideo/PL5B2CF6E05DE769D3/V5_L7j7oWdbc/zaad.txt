Trafficking Victim Tells Her Story
Uploaded on Sep 10, 2010
Child trafficking is one of the fastest growing crimes in the world. It is a modern-day form of slavery, usually associated with countries that have unstable economic and political systems. It's also a serious problem, however, in Europe, Russia and the United States - an underground business driven by enormous profits. Many victims do get help from the FAIR Fund, a Washington-based international non-profit organization that works to prevent sex and labor trafficking of young people, especially girls, around the world. In the first of two reports, one former victim, who managed to make her escape, talked about her ordeal.
