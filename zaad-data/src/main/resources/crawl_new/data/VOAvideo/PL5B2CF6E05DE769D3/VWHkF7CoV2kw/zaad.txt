Study Finds Restrictions on Free Press Intensifying Around World
Uploaded on Apr 30, 2010
The U.S.-based media rights group Freedom House says in a new study that media around the world face increasing restrictions and threats to reporters' lives. The group also says that in 2009 press freedom declined for the eighth consecutive year in row. VOA's Robert Raffaele has more.
