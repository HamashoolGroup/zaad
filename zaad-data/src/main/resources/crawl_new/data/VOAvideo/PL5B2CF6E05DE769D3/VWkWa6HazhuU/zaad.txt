AIDS Experts Gather in Vienna
Uploaded on Jul 19, 2010
The 18th International AIDS conference got underway in Vienna Sunday with the announcement of a new United Nations initiative that aims to simplify the way HIV treatment is provided so it can reach more people. VOA's Margaret Besheer reports from the Austrian capital, where on the eve of the conference AIDS activists and supporters gathered to celebrate at the Life Ball.
