Obama Eulogizes Victims of U.S. Mine Disaster
Uploaded on Apr 28, 2010
The 29 victims of America's worst coal mine disaster in decades toiled in anonymity. But they are mourned by a nation. VOA's Paula Wolfson reports at a memorial service in a West Virginia town in the heart of coal country, they were eulogized by the President of the United States.
