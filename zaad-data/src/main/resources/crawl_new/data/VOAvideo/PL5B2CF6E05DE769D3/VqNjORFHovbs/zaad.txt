President Obama's Entrepreneur Summit for Young Muslims Opens Monday
Uploaded on Apr 28, 2010
Some 250 delegates from more than 50 countries gathered in Washington Monday (April 26th), joining US officials and business leaders for a two-day Summit on Entrepreneurship. US officials say the summit will bolster ties to Muslim communities around the world. President Obama promised to hold the summit when he addressed Muslims from Cairo last year. VOA'S Ravi Khanna reports the aim is to get young entrepreneurs in the United States and the Muslim world to network.
