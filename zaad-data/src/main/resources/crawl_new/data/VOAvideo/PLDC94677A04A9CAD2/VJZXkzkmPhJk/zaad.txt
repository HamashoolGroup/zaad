Demise of America's Dinosaurs Re-Imagined in Washington
Published on Dec 8, 2014
Sixty-six million years ago, the last dinosaurs roamed what is now the American West. Their demise has now been pieced back together from the fossils they left behind, in a new exhibit at the Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History in Washington. VOA's Rosanne Skirble reports.
