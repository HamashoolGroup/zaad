A Nonprofit Develops Reliable Bees That Can Survive Winters
Published on Sep 14, 2015
The mysterious decade-long decline in the number of honeybees has prompted Master Beekeeper Billy Davis to take action. He has been keeping bees since the 1960s, and in 2013, he founded the Sustainable Honeybee Program. As Faiza Elmasry tells us, the non-profit is developing a more robust bee and teaching beekeepers how to strengthen their own colonies. Faith Lapidus narrates.
