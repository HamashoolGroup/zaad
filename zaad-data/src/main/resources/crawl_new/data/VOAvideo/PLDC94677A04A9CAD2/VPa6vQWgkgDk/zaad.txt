Global Immunizations Much Lower This Year, WHO Reports
Published on May 2, 2015
The World Health Organization observes World Immunization Week every year during the last week of April. This year, the WHO reports that immunizations are off and children are not getting the lifesaving vaccines that they need. VOA's Carol Pearson reports.
