Jessica Long - Paralympian Swimmer (pt 1)
Uploaded on Sep 5, 2008
Every four years the Paralympics bring together thousands of world-class athletes with disabilities to compete in Olympic style sporting events. The 2008 games will take place in Beijing beginning Saturday 6 September. Sixteen-year-old Jessica Long, a double amputee, is among the athletes with her sights set on a gold medal. This young swimmer was adopted by her American family from Russia and holds 14 world records. VOA's Yi Suli recently spent time with this inspiring athlete. Part 1 of 3
