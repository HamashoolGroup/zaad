In Mixed Martial Arts, Those Without Dedication Need Not Apply
Uploaded on Jul 12, 2010
Mixed martial arts - or MMA -- is a fast-moving, fast-growing sport. It combines boxing, wrestling and traditional martial arts with the leverage techniques of Brazilian jiu-jitsu. With live, monthly events held across the globe, the sport draws a worldwide audience. The fighters themselves also come from around the world -- like Iranian-born Kamal Shalorus. VOA's Arash Arabasadi spent time with Shalorus in Canada -- as he prepared for the biggest fight of his career.
