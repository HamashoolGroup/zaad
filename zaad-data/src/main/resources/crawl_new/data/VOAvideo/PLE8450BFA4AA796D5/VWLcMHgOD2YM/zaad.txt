South African Soccer Grannies Aim for Tournament in US
Uploaded on Apr 28, 2010
As South Africa prepares to host the first World Cup ever on the African continent, a group of South African women over 50 are playing in a local tournament, hoping to raise funds for an upcoming tournament in America - the Veteran's Cup. The games will be played in (the east coast state of) Massachusetts this July. Mariama Diallo reports.
