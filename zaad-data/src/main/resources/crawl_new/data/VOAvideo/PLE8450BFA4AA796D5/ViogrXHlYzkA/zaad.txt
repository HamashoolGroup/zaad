Upcoming Commonwealth Games Brings Thousands of Poor Migrant Workers' Children to New Delhi
Uploaded on Mar 4, 2010
As New Delhi prepares to host the Commonwealth Games in October, the Indian capital is undergoing an unprecedented six billion dollar building boom and beautification campaign. Much of the work is being done by a mass migration of labor into the city. And many workers come with their families. As VOA Correspondent Steve Herman reports, that has lead to many children being exposed to hazards and the lack of adequate care.
