Afghan Cricket Team Overcomes Challenges to Compete in T20 World Cup
Uploaded on May 4, 2010
Twelve teams from around the world are competing in this year's international tournament for one-day cricket matches, the Twenty20 World Cup. It is no surprise to fans that perennial powerhouses Australia, Pakistan and India are there. But this year welcomes a new addition: Afghanistan. As VOA Correspondent Sean Maroney reports from Islamabad, the Afghan team faces as many challenges off the field as it does on it.
