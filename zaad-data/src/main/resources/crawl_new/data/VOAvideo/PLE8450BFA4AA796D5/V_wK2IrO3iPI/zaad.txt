Activist Helps Build Safe Soccer Pitches
Uploaded on Nov 3, 2008
The image has been highly romanticized: children in developing countries playing the "beautiful game," soccer, barefoot in the streets. Drew Chafetz has seen, and played with, such children and seen the conditions many face: broken glass, shards of metal and trash. In the latest installment of our Making a Difference series, Brian Allen reports for VOA News on the efforts of Chafetz and his organization, love.futbol, to help young soccer lovers.
