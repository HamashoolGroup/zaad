South Africa's New, Refurbished Stadiums Await World Cup Play
Uploaded on Jun 2, 2010
As the clock ticks down for the football World Cup in South Africa, maintenance workers and groundskeepers are putting the final touches on the 10 stadiums that will host the matches. Construction on the stadiums is complete and they have been handed over to the tournament's organizers. VOA's Scott Bobb has this report.
