Political Divisions Overshadow Venezuelan Baseball Team
Uploaded on Mar 23, 2009
Organizers of the World Baseball Classic, which ended Monday, say competition is aimed at showcasing international talent and bringing the game to new fans. For the Venezuelan team, the contest has drawn unwanted attention to the country's political divide. VOA's Brian Wagner reports how some Venezuelan fans in the U.S. are venting their frustration at one top player.
