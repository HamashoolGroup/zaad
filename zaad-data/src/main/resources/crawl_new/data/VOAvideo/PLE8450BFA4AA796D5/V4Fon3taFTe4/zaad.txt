Disabled Performers Highlight Olympic Cultural Festival
Uploaded on Aug 14, 2008
The Olympic Games were created originally to bring together the best sporting talent from around the world. In recent years the scope has broadened and now the Games also showcase disabled athletes, such as at the Para-Olympics in September, and the arts, such as Beijing's Cultural Festival. VOA's Scott Bobb reports on a group that embodies both of these more recent traditions.
