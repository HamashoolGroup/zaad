Minor League Baseball Players Under Pressure
Uploaded on May 21, 2009
Baseball season is underway in the United States, and for fans this is the time to enjoy the pleasures of watching the game and rooting for their team. But for professional players on a minor league team it is a time to prove their talents in hopes of being recruited to move up and play for a team in the North American Professional Baseball League. Ernest Leong reports on how players on one minor league team are coping with the pressure to make it to the majors.
