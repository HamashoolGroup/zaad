Gospel Scion Diunna Greenleaf Belts Out the Blues
Published on Sep 9, 2015
Last month, Living Blues magazine named Diunna Greenleaf the Female Blues Artist of the Year. But while the Houston-based artist has become well-known in some other countries, she is still struggling to gain a large following in the United States. She hopes to move her career forward with some ambitious recording plans while performing whenever she can. VOA?s Greg Flakus reports.
