US Interest in Korean Culture, Other Factors, Spurs Growth in Language Study
Published on Apr 22, 2015
Korean language study is quickly growing in popularity across the United States. The Modern Language Association finds that college enrollments in Korean increased by almost 45 percent between 2009 and 2013. This is happening at a time when total language enrollments have fallen.
