Christians Hold Bible Reading Marathon on US Capitol Steps
Published on May 6, 2015
As presidents before him have done, Barack Obama is expected to issue a proclamation making Thursday a National Day of Prayer. There will be ceremonies at state capitals across the country, but one group of fervent Christians has been reading the entire bible on the steps of the U.S. Capitol in anticipation.
