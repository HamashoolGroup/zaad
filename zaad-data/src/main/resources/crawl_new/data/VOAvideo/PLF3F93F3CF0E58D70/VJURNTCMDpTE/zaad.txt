Americans Struggle to Get Families Out Amid Yemen Conflict
Published on Apr 14, 2015
Foreigners in Yemen are scrambling to get out, as clashes between government forces and the Houthi militia intensify, and a Saudi Arabia-led coalition pounds the country with airstrikes. Victoria Macchi reports from New York on what some American families are doing to help their loved ones in Yemen, including filing a lawsuit against U.S. Secretary of State John Kerry.
