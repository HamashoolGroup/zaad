Published on Jan 11, 2015
Secretary of State John Kerry says he is convinced that if the United States and India work together, the two countries can help forge a ?new era of shared possibility? that could benefit millions of people in India and across Asia. He commented at an Indian investment forum, hosted by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. VOA State Department correspondent Pam Dockins has the story.
