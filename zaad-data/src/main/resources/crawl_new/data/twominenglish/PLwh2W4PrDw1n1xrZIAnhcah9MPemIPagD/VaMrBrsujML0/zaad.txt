Making an Appointment in English - Learn to speak English in Common Situations
Published on Feb 21, 2013
If you are working you may often need to meet people to discuss professional business. When you do that you need to make sure that they are available at the time you want to meet them. This is called making an appointment. In this lesson we will learn the words and the phrases that we can use when we make an appointment with someone.
