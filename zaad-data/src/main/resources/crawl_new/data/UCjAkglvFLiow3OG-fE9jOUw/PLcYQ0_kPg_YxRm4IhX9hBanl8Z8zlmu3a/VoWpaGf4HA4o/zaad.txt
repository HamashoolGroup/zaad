--- VIDEO TITLE: Simple Present Tense (Present Simple) - English Lesson with inlingua Vancouver --- VIDEO DESCRIPTION: Simple Present Tense (Present Simple) For of the verb which shows the action/state takes place in the present. When to use: 1 - Repeating action or habits 2 - Facts or things which are true. 3 - Conditions (Not Actions) taking place at this moment. 1 - Repeating actions or habits: He goes to school by bus. They study English every day. Useful time expressions: Always, usually, often, rarely, never, etc. 2 - Facts!! 100% true! e.g. inlingua Vancouver is in Vancouver, Canada. Cows eat grass 3 - Conditions (Not Actions) taking place at this moment. e.g. She likes English. They feel tired. This is a good lesson. --- TEACHER: Linda Connect with us!
