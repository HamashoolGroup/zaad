--- VIDEO TITLE: Even though, though and although - English Lessons with inlingua Vancouver --- VIDEO DESCRIPTION: Though, although, even though Though, although and even though are words that let us know about a cause, and a consequence that is contrasting. Basically, they do the job of the conjunction 'but', and are used when the contrasting result needs to be emphasized. It is important to broaden our vocabulary when it comes to different ways to express conjunctions, especially in our writing. Though, although and even though are some of the ways you can express the idea of 'but'. Watch this video to see how they can be used. Connect with us!
