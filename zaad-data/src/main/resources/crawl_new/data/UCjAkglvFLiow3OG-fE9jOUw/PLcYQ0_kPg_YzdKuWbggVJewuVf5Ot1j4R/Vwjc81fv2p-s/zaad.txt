--- VIDEO TITLE: Polite Pronouns - Using 'you' - English Lessons with inlingua Vancouver It is polite to put yourself second Wrong: Me and Mike want to go to town. Right but impolite: I and Mike want to go to town. Right and polite: Mike and I want to go to town. 'You' is everywhere! But is it formal or informal? Informal: If you add one and one, you get two. Formal: One plus one equals two. Informal: If you're late, you'll get in trouble. Formal: Students who are late get in trouble. Informal: You have to pay taxes.
