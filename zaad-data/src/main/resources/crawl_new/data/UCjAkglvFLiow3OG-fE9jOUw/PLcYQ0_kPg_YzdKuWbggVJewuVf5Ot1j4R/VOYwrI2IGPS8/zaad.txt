--- VIDEO TITLE: Pronouns pointers - English Lessons with inlingua Vancouver What is a pronoun? A word that stands in for a noun Without pronouns: Parker gave Parker's dog the dog bath. Parker gave her dog its bath. Use pronouns to: Name people or things: you look like him. Point to non-specific people or things: Everyone enjoyed the party. To point to something. This is the dress I want. To refer back to the subject: Bruce hurt himself. To show mutual action: Ryan and Wesley were wrestling and hurt each other. To add emphasis: I myself love intensive pronouns. To ask a question: Who is that? To show ownership: That's not yours; it's mine. --- TEACHER: TASH Connect with us!
