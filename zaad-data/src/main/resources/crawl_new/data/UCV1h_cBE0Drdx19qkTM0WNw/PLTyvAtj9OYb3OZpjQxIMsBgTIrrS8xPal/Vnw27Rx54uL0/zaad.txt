Learn English Conversation For Beginners (100 Lessons). Lesson 12 - Do you have enough money? Joe : Laura, what are you going to do today? Laura : I'm going shopping. Joe : What time are you leaving? Laura : 25 dollars. Do you think that's enough? Joe : That's not very much. Laura : I think it's OK. I also have two credit cards. Joe : Let me give you another ten dollars. Laura : Thanks.
