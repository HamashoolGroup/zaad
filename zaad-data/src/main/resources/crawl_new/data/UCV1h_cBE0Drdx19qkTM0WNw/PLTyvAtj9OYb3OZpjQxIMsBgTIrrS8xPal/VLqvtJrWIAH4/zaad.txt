Learn English Conversation For Beginners (100 Lessons). Lesson 4: Asking Directions. A: Hi Michael. B: Hi Amy. What's up? A: I'm looking for the airport. Can you tell me how to get there? A: Oh, I see it now. Thanks. B: No problem. A: Do you know if there's a restroom around here? B: Yes, there's one here. It's in the store. A: Thank you. B: Bye.
