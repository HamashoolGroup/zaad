Communication skills. - Personality development Skills - part 9. http://www.letstalk.co.in All human exchange, personal or professional is based on communication. It is our responsibility to make others understand. It is vital to be able to communicate and interact effectively with others using both verbal and non-verbal communication. Communication is an ongoing process. Derived from Latin word ' communis', which means ' to share' , the word communication means sharing ideas, messages and words, expressed through a language. Communication is a two-way process. It requires understanding and proper feedback. Hence for any interaction to turn into communication, we need a communicator and a receiver.
