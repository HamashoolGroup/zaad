ob Interview Skills Training Part 4.2 - Body Language in an interview. Important tips for body language during an interview. Walk smart but modestly from the entrance to the table. Move your right hand forward while shaking hand. SIT standard in a crossed legged position. Maintain a moderate eye contact. Do not shake your hands and head aimlessly. Do not do anything distractive. Do not place your hands on the table. Do not lean back on the chair. Pick up your papers on receiving signals from the chairperson. Stand and thank them before leaving.
