Published on Nov 13, 2015
The Islamic State group has claimed responsibility for two suicide bomb blasts in a Shiite dominated suburb of Beirut that have killed at least 37 people and wounded others. The suicide bombings hit a community centre and a nearby bakery in a crowded commercial and residential area in Beirut's southern suburbs. The area is a seen as a stronghold of the militant Hezbollah group.
