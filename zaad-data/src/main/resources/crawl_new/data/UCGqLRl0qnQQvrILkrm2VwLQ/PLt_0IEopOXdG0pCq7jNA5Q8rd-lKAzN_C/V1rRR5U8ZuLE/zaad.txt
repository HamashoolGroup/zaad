Present continuous and present simple Present Simple We use the present simple tense for: things that we do regularly, facts, habits, truths and permanent situations Example I check my email every day. (regular activity) Present Continuous We use the present continuous tense for: things that are happening at the time we are speaking, temporary situations and activities that are in progress Example Just a minute. I'm checking my email. (now)
