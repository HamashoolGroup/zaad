I have / eat my breakfast I have a shower I have a wash / I wash my face I have a shave I do my make up / I put on my make up I do my teeth / I clean my teeth / I brush my teeth I comb my hair / I brush my hair I get dressed I go out / I leave the house / I leave for school / I leave for work I arrive at work / I arrive at school I have lunch at... I leave school at... / I leave work at I get back home / I get in I have / eat dinner I go to the gym I watch television I go to football practice / dance practice I get ready for bed I do / clean / brush my teeth I go to bed Useful words About Around Approximately Between On the dot Sharp Usually Normally Example Every working day, I get up around seven in the morning. I go downstairs and have breakfast. At quarter to eight I have a wash, shave, brush my teeth and put my make up on. I leave the house at ten past eight and arrive at work at half past. I have lunch for 30 minutes around one and leave work between half four and five. I get home at five or just after and eat dinner at six. In the evenings, I usually work on some stuff on my computer for a bit and watch television or sports for a while. I go to bed by 11. At the weekend, I wake up later, normally about eight or nine.
