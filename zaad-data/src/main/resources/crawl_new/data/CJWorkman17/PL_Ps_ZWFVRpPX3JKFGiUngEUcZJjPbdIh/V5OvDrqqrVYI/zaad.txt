Learn British English Free: Recycling (rubbish bins)
Published on Sep 3, 2015
In this special lesson, I explain the British recycling system. Black / grey bin: general rubbish (not to be recycled) Green bin: natural waste (e.g. grass; flower cuttings) Blue bin: recycling (plastic bottles; glass; cardboard boxes) These videos on my Youtube channel provide free educational material about British English, British pronunciation and British accent training.
