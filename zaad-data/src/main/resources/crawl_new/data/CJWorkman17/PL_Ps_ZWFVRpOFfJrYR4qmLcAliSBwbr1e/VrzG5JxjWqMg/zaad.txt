Each constituency votes in a Member of Parliament (MP). Each individual votes for their candidate of choice in their constituency only. Winning party must get at least 312 MPs elected (approximately). Winning party therefore gets most individual constituency wins. Consider local voting vs national voting issues. Tories fell short and had to ask the Liberal Democrats to join them. General elections every five years.
