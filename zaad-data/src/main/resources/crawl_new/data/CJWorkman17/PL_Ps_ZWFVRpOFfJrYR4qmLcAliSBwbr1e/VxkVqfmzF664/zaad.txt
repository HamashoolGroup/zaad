British English accent training lesson 16: religion
Published on Oct 21, 2012
In this lesson I discuss some of the religions from around the world. I am not promoting any individual religion. Please see the notes below for spelling and pronunciation phonetics (in brackets), and see this BBC website about religion for more information: http://www.bbc.co.uk/religion/religions/ These videos on my Youtube channel provide free educational material about British English, British pronunciation and British accent training.
