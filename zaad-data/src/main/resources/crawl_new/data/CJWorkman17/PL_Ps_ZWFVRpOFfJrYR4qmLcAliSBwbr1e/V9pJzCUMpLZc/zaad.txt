Famous Accents London: Cockney Birmingham: Brummy Liverpool: Scouse Newcastle: Geordie Glasgow: Glaswegian Somerset / Dorset: West country Others: Yorkshire, Manchester, Wales, Scotland, Ireland Received pronunciation RP is defined in the Concise Oxford Dictionary as "the standard accent of English as spoken in the south of England". If you watch my English accent training videos, that describes how I speak -- in received pronunciation. http://www.bl.uk/learning/langlit/sou... Phonetics Phonetics refers to writing a word the way that it is pronounced as opposed to the correct grammatical spelling. The adverb is that when terms are spelt phonetically.
