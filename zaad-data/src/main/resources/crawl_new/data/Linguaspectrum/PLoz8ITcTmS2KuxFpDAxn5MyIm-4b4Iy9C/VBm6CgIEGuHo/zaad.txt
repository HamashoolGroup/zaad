The British English Expression - Dreaded Lurgy The dreaded lurgy is an illness that is not too serious but which is very easy to catch. I wouldn't mind coming down with the dreaded lurgy if those nurses at the hospital were looking after me. It's almost worth catching the dreaded lurgy to be looked after by those fit nurses.
