Lola English Lesson 4: Loud, Quiet, Fast, and Slow
Uploaded on Feb 27, 2010
Lola demonstrates her percussion skills and the difference between loud vs. quiet and fast vs. slow LISTEN FOR THESE QUESTIONS: Can you show us loud and quiet? Which one first? Can you play slow? Now, what? Can you play louder? Can you sing it? LISTEN FOR THESE COLLOS: Let's see, that's very loud, that's fast, so loud, really loud, let's do that LISTEN FOR SHRINKING AND LINKING (when words in English sound shorter and connect together): You're gonna play drums? Kin you play louder? Kin you sing it? Lessee loud. Oh you wanna play Kitty Cat Lying in the Sun?
