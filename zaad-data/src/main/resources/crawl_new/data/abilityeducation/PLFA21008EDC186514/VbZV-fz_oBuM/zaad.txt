Tips on how to use the new ABILITY English brochure
Published on Nov 3, 2013
Ruby Biscuit, International Strategy Manager, talks through the features of the new ABILITY English brochure 2014. ABILITY English is an English language college with campuses in Sydney and Melbourne. The brochure is designed to explain ABILITY English courses and campuses to students and international education agents. Ruby outlines the different sections of the brochure and the use of new icons. The aim of these new features is to make it easier for agents to help students make the right decision on which course is best for them. To learn more visit our website www.ability.edu.au or contact us directly at info@ability.edu.au
