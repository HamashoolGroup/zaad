Survivor English ? Gain confidence speaking English in real situations
Published on Oct 9, 2014
You may often feel fearful or shy speaking English with locals, so in this course we teach you how to overcome your fears and connect. Every day we teach you practical communication skills with lots of detailed feedback from your teacher, so you are more confidently prepared to manage and control real-life situations that challenge you. We give you opportunities to practise common, real-life communication using the appropriate language with English speakers in a variety of environments. Step out of your comfort zone and into conversation with the world. For more information visit http://www.ability.edu.au/our-english...
