ABILITY Film Night 2013 - Melbourne
On the 4th of July (America's Independence Day) ABILITY English Melbourne City Campus had their annual Film Night at Beer Deluxe in Federation Square. There were lots of students, teachers and student services staff and everyone had a GREAT TIME!!!!! There were at least 140 people on the night. There were 11 different films.
