In today's video, I broke down for you the SAT Vocabulary word, REDOLENT, to help you memorize it. REDOLENT (adj): Smelling of; giving off odors Synonyms: Aromatic, perfumed, fragrant, scented Sentence: The cup was redolent with the smell of orange juice. How to Memorize the SAT Word, REDOLENT: The key idea with being able to quickly memorize english vocabulary words is the create pictures for those words. The Pictures for this SAT Vocabulary Word is: REDOLENT: Red Deodorant Definition: Smelly Girl Then just take both the picture for the word and the picture for the definition and visualize an interaction between the two. Story: Girl puts on red deodorant on smelly friend That's It! Just review a little bit and you will have these words fully memorized come test day.
