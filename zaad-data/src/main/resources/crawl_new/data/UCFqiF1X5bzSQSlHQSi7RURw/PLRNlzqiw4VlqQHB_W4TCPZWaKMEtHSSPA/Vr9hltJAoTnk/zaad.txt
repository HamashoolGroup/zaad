Here is the definition for the word: PLACID (adj): Calm or Peaceful Synonyms: Calm, serene, mild, cool, unruffled, levelheaded Sentence: The student gave a speech in a very placid tone of voice. The key idea with being able to quickly memorize english vocabulary words is the create pictures for those words. The Pictures for this SAT Vocabulary Word is: PLACID: Plastic Bottle of Water Definition: Meditating Calm Monk Then just take both the picture for the word and the picture for the definition and visualize an interaction between the two. Story: Meditating CALM Monk floating in a PLASTIC Bottle of Water Next word: TALISMAN (Noun): Lucky Charm; object that brings good fortune Synonyms: Good-luck piece, lucky item, rabbit's foot Sentence: Ever since the young girl found the talisman at the beach, she has been getting straight A's in school. The Pictures for this SAT Vocabulary Word is: TALISMAN: Tallest Man Definition: Lucky Charm Pendant Story: The TALLEST MAN grows Taller by Rubbing his LUCKY CHARM PENDANT. That's It!
