Advice Advise Advice has a /s/ sound Advice Advise has a /z/, voiced sound. Advise Advice Advice is a noun. It means an opinion. If I give you my advice, I'm giving you my opinion. Advise is a verb. To give my opinion or give your opinion. I advised him not to go there My advice to him was that he shouldn't go there. I advise you to learn English. My advice to you is to learn English.
