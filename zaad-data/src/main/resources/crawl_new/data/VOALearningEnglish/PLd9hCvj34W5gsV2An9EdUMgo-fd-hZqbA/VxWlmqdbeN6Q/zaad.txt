Out of Aleppo: A Syrian Tells His Story
Published on Oct 18, 2013
TALK2US Extra: "Ahmad," an engineer, first called the show in February 2012, hours after two suicide bombings at security buildings shattered the relative calm of the northern city. He says he recently left Syria and is now in Spain, but his mother and sister are still in Aleppo, and he tells VOA's Avi Arditti that "I still feel the fear."
